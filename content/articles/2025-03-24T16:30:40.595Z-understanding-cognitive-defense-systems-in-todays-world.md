## Common Challenges and Solutions in Cognitive Security System Implementation

Implementing a cognitive defense system presents unique challenges:

* **Skill Gap:**  Managing and interpreting the insights generated by these systems requires specialized expertise. **Solution:** Invest in training and upskilling your security team.

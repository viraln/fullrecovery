## Adaptive Design Biomimicry: Key Principles and Processes

The core of adaptive biomimicry lies in understanding and implementing these key principles:

* **Functionality over Form:** Focus on the *how* rather than the *what*.  What processes allow an organism to adapt?
* **Systems Thinking:** Consider the interconnectedness of components and their interaction with the environment.
* **Iterative Design:** Embrace experimentation and continuous improvement, mimicking nature's evolutionary process.
* **Multi-Disciplinary Collaboration:**  Biologists, engineers, designers, and material scientists working together are crucial for success.

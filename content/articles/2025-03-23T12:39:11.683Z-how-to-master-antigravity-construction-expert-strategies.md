## Antigravity Construction Methods: A Paradigm Shift

The methods used in antigravity construction will necessitate a radical departure from traditional techniques.  Current research suggests several promising approaches:

1. **Modular Construction:**  Prefabricated modules assembled on the ground and then lifted into position using maglev or other antigravity technologies.
2. **3D Printing in Space:**  Utilizing 3D printing technologies in space, with materials delivered by specialized spacecraft, could significantly reduce transportation costs and limitations.
3. **In-situ Resource Utilization (ISRU):**  Using materials found on-site (e.g., lunar regolith) to build structures on the moon or other celestial bodies.  This is a key aspect of space-based antigravity construction.

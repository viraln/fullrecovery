## Common Challenges and Solutions

Despite the significant progress, several challenges remain in the development of quantum entanglement relays:

*   **Loss:** Photons carrying quantum information can be lost in optical fibers or other transmission channels.

*   **Complexity:** Building and operating quantum entanglement relays is a complex engineering challenge.

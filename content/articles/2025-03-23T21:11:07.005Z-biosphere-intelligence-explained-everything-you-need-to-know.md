##  Closed Ecological Systems Intelligence: A Microcosm of BI

Closed ecological systems (CES), like Biosphere 2, provide ideal testbeds for BI research. These self-contained environments allow scientists to meticulously control variables and observe the effects of AI interventions in a controlled setting.

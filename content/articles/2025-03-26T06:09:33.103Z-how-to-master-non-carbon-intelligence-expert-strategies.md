## Understanding the Landscape of Non-Carbon Intelligence

Non-Carbon Intelligence (NCI) isn't just about reducing the carbon footprint of AI; it's about fundamentally rethinking how we design, train, and deploy AI systems.  It encompasses a range of approaches, including the development of more energy-efficient algorithms, the use of renewable energy sources for AI infrastructure, and the application of AI to solve climate change challenges.  The goal is to create a virtuous cycle where AI becomes a powerful tool for environmental sustainability, rather than a contributor to the problem.

> **EXPERT TIP:**  Focusing solely on reducing the energy consumption of AI models is insufficient.  NCI requires a holistic approach considering the entire lifecycle of an AI system, from data collection to deployment and decommissioning.

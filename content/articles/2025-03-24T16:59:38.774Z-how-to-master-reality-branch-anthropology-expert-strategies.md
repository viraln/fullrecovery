## Key Concepts and Theoretical Frameworks in RBA

Understanding RBA requires grasping several core concepts:

* **Reality Branching:** The idea that multiple realities, differing in significant ways, coexist. This isn't necessarily about multiverse theories, but rather the recognition of diverse subjective experiences and their impact on culture.
* **Reality Anchors:**  The stabilizing factors within a reality branchâ€”shared beliefs, technologies, or social structures that maintain its coherence.
* **Branch Convergence/Divergence:** The interplay between different reality branches, whether they merge, separate, or influence each other.

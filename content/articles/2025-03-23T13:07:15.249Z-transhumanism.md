## Transhumanism vs. Posthumanism: A Subtle Distinction

While often used interchangeably, transhumanism and posthumanism represent distinct concepts. Transhumanism focuses on using technology to *enhance* human capabilities, whereas posthumanism envisions a future where humans have *transcended* their biological limitations to such a degree that they are fundamentally different from humans as we know them.  Posthumanism often involves a blurring of the lines between human and machine intelligence.  ![A comparison chart illustrating the key differences between transhumanism and posthumanism.](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1484417894907-623942c8ee29?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress)

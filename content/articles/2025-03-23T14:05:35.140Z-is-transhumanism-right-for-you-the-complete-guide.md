## What is Transhumanism? Understanding the Movement

Transhumanism is a philosophical and intellectual movement that advocates for the use of technology to enhance human intellectual, physical, and psychological capacities.  It envisions a future where humans transcend their current biological limitations through advancements in areas like biotechnology, nanotechnology, information technology, and cognitive science.  The transhumanist movement isn't about replacing humanity; it's about *improving* it.  It's about empowering individuals to take control of their own evolution. ![A futuristic image depicting enhanced humans](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1451187580459-43490279c0fa?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress)

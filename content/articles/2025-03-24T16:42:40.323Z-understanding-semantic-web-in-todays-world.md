## What is the Semantic Web?

The Semantic Web builds upon the existing World Wide Web by adding a layer of meaning.  Instead of simply presenting information, it focuses on making that information understandable by machines. This is achieved through standardized vocabularies (ontologies) and metadata that describe the relationships between different pieces of data. ## Common Challenges and Solutions

Despite its potential, the Semantic Web faces some challenges:

## Meaning Markets Business Model:  Profit with Purpose

The Meaning Markets business model differs significantly from traditional models.  While profit remains essential, it's interwoven with a strong social or environmental mission.  This often involves:

1. **Transparency:** Openly communicating sourcing, production, and impact metrics.

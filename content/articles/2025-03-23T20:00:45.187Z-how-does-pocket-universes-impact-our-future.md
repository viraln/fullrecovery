## Understanding the Pocket Universe Theory

The pocket universe theory, a branch of cosmology, proposes the existence of self-contained universes separate from our own.  These aren't just tiny versions of our universe; they could have different physical constants, dimensions, and laws of physics. ![Artistic representation of a pocket universe emerging from a larger universe](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1481174228429-b2c3f5680a7a?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress)  The most common theoretical framework for pocket universes arises from the inflationary multiverse theory, which suggests our universe is just one bubble in a vast, ever-expanding sea of other universes. Some theories even propose that these universes could be created through quantum fluctuations or advanced technologies.

## 1. Unmatched Resilience and Redundancy: The Self-Healing Network

Traditional star network topologies, often used in smart city initiatives, suffer from a critical vulnerability: a single point of failure. If the central hub goes down, the entire system collapses.  Smart infrastructure mesh networks, however, offer unparalleled resilience.  Their decentralized, self-organizing nature means that if one node fails, the network automatically reroutes traffic around the obstruction, ensuring continuous operation.  This inherent redundancy is crucial for critical infrastructure like emergency services, traffic management, and power grids.  The implications are profound: uninterrupted service, increased reliability, and a significant reduction in downtime.

> **EXPERT TIP:** When designing your smart infrastructure mesh, consider incorporating diverse communication protocols (e.g., LoRaWAN, Wi-Fi, cellular) for maximum resilience against potential disruptions in any single technology.

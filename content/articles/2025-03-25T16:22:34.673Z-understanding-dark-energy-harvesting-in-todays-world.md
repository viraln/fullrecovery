## The Nature of Dark Energy: Unpacking the Mystery

Dark energy constitutes approximately 68% of the universe's total energy density.  Its existence is inferred from observations of accelerating cosmic expansion, a phenomenon first discovered in the late 1990s.  ![A depiction of the accelerating expansion of the universe](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1451847251646-8a6c0dd1510c?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress) Unlike ordinary matter and dark matter, which exert gravitational attraction, dark energy exhibits a repulsive gravitational effect, pushing galaxies apart.  Its precise nature remains one of the biggest unsolved mysteries in modern physics. Leading theories suggest it's a property of spacetime itself, or perhaps a new type of energy field we don't yet fully comprehend.

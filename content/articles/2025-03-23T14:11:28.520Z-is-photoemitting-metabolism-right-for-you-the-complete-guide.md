## Understanding Photoemitting Metabolism: Bioluminescence and Beyond

Photoemitting metabolism, often synonymous with bioluminescence metabolism, leverages the natural light-producing capabilities of certain organisms. Scientists harness this natural phenomenon by introducing luciferase or similar reporters into cells or organisms of interest.

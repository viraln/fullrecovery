## What is a Neural Collective?

A neural collective is a system of multiple, often heterogeneous, AI agents that communicate and cooperate to achieve a common goal. Unlike traditional AI systems that function in isolation, a neural collective leverages the power of distributed intelligence, mirroring the intricate workings of the human brain or the collaborative nature of ant colonies.  Each agent within the collective specializes in a particular task or possesses unique data, contributing its expertise to the overall problem-solving process.

## What is Time Banking?

Time banking is a reciprocal system where individuals exchange their skills and services with others on a time-based, rather than monetary, basis. One hour of your time is worth one hour of someone else's, creating a community-based network of mutual support.  Instead of using money, members accumulate "time credits" for the services they provide, which they can then redeem for services they need from other members. This fosters a sense of community, reduces social isolation, and empowers individuals to contribute their unique talents. ![A diverse group of people participating in various time banking activities](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1434030216411-0b793f4b4173?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress)

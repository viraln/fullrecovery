## AI vs. Machine Learning:  Understanding the Nuances

While often used interchangeably, AI and machine learning are distinct concepts.  AI is the broader concept encompassing the creation of intelligent machines, while machine learning is a specific subset of AI techniques that enable systems to learn from data without explicit programming. Deep learning, in turn, is a more advanced subset of machine learning.

| Feature        | AI                               | Machine Learning                     | Deep Learning                         |

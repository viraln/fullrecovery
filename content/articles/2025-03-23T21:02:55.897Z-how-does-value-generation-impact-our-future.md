## Measuring Value Generation: Key Metrics and KPIs

Measuring value generation requires a move beyond traditional financial metrics. While profitability remains important, it's crucial to incorporate metrics that reflect the broader impact:

* **Customer Satisfaction (CSAT):** Measures how satisfied customers are with your product or service.
* **Net Promoter Score (NPS):**  Gauges customer loyalty and willingness to recommend your business.
* **Employee Engagement:**  Measures employee satisfaction and commitment.
* **Environmental Impact:**  Tracks your company's carbon footprint and other environmental metrics.
* **Social Impact:**  Quantifies the positive social contributions of your business.

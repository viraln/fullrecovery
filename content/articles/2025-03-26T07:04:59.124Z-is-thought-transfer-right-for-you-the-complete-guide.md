## What is Thought Transfer and How Does it Work?

Thought transfer, also known as mind-to-mind communication or telepathy, refers to the direct transmission of thoughts, feelings, or images from one mind to another without the use of conventional sensory channels like speech or writing.  While widely considered a paranormal phenomenon in the past, modern research approaches thought transfer through a neurotechnological lens.  Current efforts focus on decoding brain activity and translating it into signals that can be transmitted and interpreted by another brain. ![Illustration of brain signals being transmitted wirelessly](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1532798442725-41036acc7489?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress)

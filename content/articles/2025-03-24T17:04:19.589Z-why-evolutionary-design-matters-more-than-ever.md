## The Evolutionary Design Process: A Step-by-Step Guide

The evolutionary design process is not a rigid formula but a flexible framework.  However, key steps typically include:

**Step 1:** Define the problem and establish clear goals. What are you trying to achieve?
**Step 2:** Generate a diverse set of initial design concepts (ideation phase).
**Step 3:** Prototype these concepts, creating tangible representations (low-fidelity initially).
**Step 4:** Test and evaluate prototypes through user feedback and data analysis.

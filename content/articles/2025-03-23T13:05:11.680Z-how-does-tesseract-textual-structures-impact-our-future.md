## Tesseract OCR Accuracy Improvement:  Strategies and Techniques

While Tesseract boasts impressive accuracy, achieving optimal results often requires fine-tuning. Key strategies include:

* **Pre-processing:** Cleaning images by removing noise, enhancing contrast, and correcting skew significantly improves accuracy.
* **Language Selection:** Specifying the correct language during text extraction is crucial.
* **Choosing the Right Tesseract Engine:** Experimenting with different engine modes and configurations can yield better outcomes for specific types of documents.
* **Post-processing:** Employing techniques like spell checking and contextual correction can further enhance the accuracy of extracted text.

## How Does Food AI Work?

At the heart of Food AI lies machine learning (ML) and deep learning (DL). These techniques enable AI systems to learn from vast amounts of data, identify patterns, and make predictions.  For example:

1. **Data Collection:** Sensors, cameras, and other devices collect data on various aspects of food production and consumption (e.g., soil conditions, crop yields, consumer preferences).
2. **Data Processing:** This data is cleaned, processed, and prepared for analysis using various techniques.
3. **Model Training:**  Machine learning algorithms are trained on this data to identify patterns and relationships.
4. **Prediction and Optimization:** The trained models are used to make predictions (e.g., predicting crop yields, optimizing delivery routes) and suggest actions to improve efficiency and reduce waste.
5. **Feedback and Iteration:** The system continuously learns and improves based on real-world feedback.

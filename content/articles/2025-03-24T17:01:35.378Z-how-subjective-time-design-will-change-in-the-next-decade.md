## Designing for Flow: The Key to Positive Subjective Time

The concept of "flow state," a state of deep engagement and immersion, is central to positive subjective time design.  When users are in a flow state, they lose track of time because they're fully absorbed in the activity.  Designing for flow involves:

1. **Clear goals and immediate feedback:** Users need to understand their objectives and receive regular feedback on their progress.
2. **Challenging yet achievable tasks:**  The difficulty level should be appropriately matched to the user's skill level to maintain engagement.
3. **Focused attention:** Minimize distractions and ensure the interface is intuitive and easy to navigate.

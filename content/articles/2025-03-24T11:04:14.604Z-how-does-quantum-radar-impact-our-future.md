Traditional radar relies on transmitting radio waves and detecting their reflections.  Quantum radar, however, leverages the bizarre yet powerful principles of quantum mechanics. Instead of relying on the reflection of classical electromagnetic waves, quantum radar can detect subtle changes in the quantum state of photons interacting with a target.

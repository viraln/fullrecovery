## Designing and Building Synthetic Ecosystems: A Practical Guide

Building a synthetic ecosystem isn't a simple task. It requires careful planning and execution.  Here's a simplified overview of the process:

1. **Define the Goal:**  Clearly outline the desired function of the synthetic ecosystem (e.g., bioremediation, bioproduction).
2. **Species Selection:**  Choose organisms with the desired traits and interactions.  This often involves genetic engineering and modification.

## Benefits of a Digital Constitution

The advantages of embracing a digital constitution are numerous and far-reaching:

* **Increased Transparency and Accountability:**  Citizens can easily access and understand their constitutional rights and the actions of their government.
* **Enhanced Citizen Participation:**  Digital platforms can facilitate greater public engagement in the legislative process through surveys, forums, and online debates.

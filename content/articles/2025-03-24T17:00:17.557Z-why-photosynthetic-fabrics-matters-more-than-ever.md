## How Does Photosynthetic Fabric Work?

Photosynthetic fabrics incorporate living photosynthetic microorganisms, such as algae or cyanobacteria, directly into the fabric structure. ![Microscopic image of algae within a fabric sample](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1540292370723-60cf448886c3?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress)  These microscopic powerhouses capture sunlight and, through photosynthesis, convert carbon dioxide into oxygen and organic compounds.

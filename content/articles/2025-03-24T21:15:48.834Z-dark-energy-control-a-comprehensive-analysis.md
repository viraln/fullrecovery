## Understanding Dark Energy: The Basics

Dark energy, first observed through the accelerating expansion of the universe, remains one of the greatest enigmas in modern cosmology.  Its repulsive gravitational effect counteracts the attractive force of gravity, driving the universe's expansion at an ever-increasing rate. While we can observe its effects, its fundamental nature is still unknown.  Leading theories propose it's a property of space itself (cosmological constant) or a dynamic entity (quintessence). ![A graph illustrating the accelerating expansion of the universe](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1475070929565-c985b496cb9f?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress)

## What is Multispecies Justice? A Definition and Theoretical Framework

Multispecies justice, at its core, is a theoretical and practical framework that challenges the hierarchical structures of power that privilege human interests over those of other species.  It rejects the anthropocentric view that humans are inherently superior and argues for a more equitable distribution of resources and opportunities across the entire web of life.  The **multispecies justice definition** encompasses the recognition of the intrinsic value of all living beings and the dismantling of systems that cause harm and injustice to non-human species. ![A diverse ecosystem showcasing various species](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1451187580459-43490279c0fa?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress)

## Microeconomics vs. Macroeconomics: Two Sides of the Same Coin

Economics is broadly divided into two branches:

| Feature          | Microeconomics                               | Macroeconomics                                   |
|-----------------|-----------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------|
| **Focus**        | Individual economic agents (consumers, firms) | The economy as a whole (national and global levels) |
| **Examples**     | Supply and demand of a specific product, consumer behavior, market structures | Inflation, unemployment, economic growth, fiscal and monetary policy |
| **Analytical Tools** | Demand curves, cost functions, game theory    | Aggregate demand and supply, national income accounting |

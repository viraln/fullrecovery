## Understanding Human-Wildlife Conflict and its Impacts

Human-wildlife conflict (HWC) is a pervasive issue, impacting both human livelihoods and wildlife populations globally.  It arises when the needs and activities of humans and wildlife overlap, leading to negative interactions. These interactions range from crop raiding by elephants to livestock predation by wolves, and even human injury or death from encounters with dangerous animals.  The consequences are far-reaching: economic losses for farmers, injuries and fatalities for humans, and habitat loss and population decline for wildlife.  ![A graphic showing the various types of human-wildlife conflict](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1516934024742-b461fba47600?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress)

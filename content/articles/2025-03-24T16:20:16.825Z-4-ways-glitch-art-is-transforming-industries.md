## What is Glitch Art? A Deep Dive

Glitch art, at its core, is the aesthetic exploration of digital errors.  It leverages software malfunctions, corrupted data, and manipulated code to create unique and often mesmerizing visual effects.  Think of it as a deliberate embrace of the unexpected, transforming technical glitches into intentional artistic expressions.  ![Example of vibrant, abstract glitch art](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1598029018509-3cceeec6faba?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress)

Unlike accidental glitches, glitch artists actively seek out and manipulate these errors, using various software and techniques to control and refine the final aesthetic.

## Common Challenges and Potential Solutions

Despite its potential, underground urbanism faces several challenges:

* **High construction costs:**  Building underground is inherently more expensive than above-ground construction.
* **Ventilation and air quality:**  Ensuring proper ventilation and air quality in enclosed underground spaces is crucial.
* **Safety and security:**  Implementing robust safety and security measures to protect against hazards and emergencies.
* **Accessibility and inclusivity:**  Designing underground spaces to be accessible to people with disabilities.

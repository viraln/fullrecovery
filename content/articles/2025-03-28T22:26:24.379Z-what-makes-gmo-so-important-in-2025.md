## Risks of GMOs: Addressing Legitimate Concerns

While the scientific consensus supports the safety of GMOs, it's important to acknowledge legitimate concerns.

*   **Potential for Allergenicity:** There is a theoretical risk that GMOs could introduce new allergens into the food supply. However, rigorous testing is conducted to minimize this risk.
*   **Development of Herbicide-Resistant Weeds:** The widespread use of herbicide-tolerant crops has led to the emergence of herbicide-resistant weeds, requiring farmers to use more toxic herbicides.
*   **Impact on Biodiversity:** Some worry that GMOs could negatively impact biodiversity by displacing native plant species.

Addressing these concerns requires ongoing research, responsible regulation, and sustainable farming practices.

##  Predictive Modeling and Personalized Prevention Strategies

The future of high-altitude illness prevention lies in personalized medicine.  Researchers are actively exploring genetic markers that predict susceptibility to altitude sickness. This predictive modeling, combined with advanced algorithms analyzing factors like acclimatization rate and environmental conditions, will enable the development of highly personalized prevention strategies, significantly reducing the incidence of altitude-related illnesses.

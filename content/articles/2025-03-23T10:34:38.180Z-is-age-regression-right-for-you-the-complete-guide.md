## What is Age Regression?

Age regression, in its simplest form, is a process of mentally and emotionally returning to a younger state of being.  This isn't about literally becoming a child again; rather, it's about accessing the mindset and emotional landscape of a younger self.  This can be achieved through various techniques, ranging from guided imagery and visualization to role-playing and the use of specific comfort objects.  ![A serene image depicting a person peacefully engaging in a relaxation technique](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1534768654272-e97681c3a2c7?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress).

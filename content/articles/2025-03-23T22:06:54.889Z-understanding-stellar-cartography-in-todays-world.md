## Common Challenges and Solutions in Stellar Cartography

Despite significant advancements, challenges remain:

* **Light pollution:**  Urban light pollution significantly obscures the night sky, making accurate observations difficult.  Solutions include seeking dark-sky locations or utilizing specialized filters.
* **Atmospheric distortion:**  The Earth's atmosphere can distort the apparent positions of stars.  Advanced software can compensate for this, but it's crucial to consider this factor.
* **Data processing:**  Analyzing massive datasets from space telescopes requires sophisticated algorithms and computational power.

##  Naval Intelligence Gathering:  Strategies and Best Practices

Naval intelligence gathering requires a coordinated effort across multiple agencies and platforms.  Effective strategies include:

1. **Signal Intelligence (SIGINT):** Intercepting and analyzing communications from naval vessels and other maritime entities.
2. **Human Intelligence (HUMINT):** Gathering information through human sources, such as informants and defectors.
3. **Open-Source Intelligence (OSINT):** Leveraging publicly available information from websites, news reports, and social media.
4. **Measurement and Signature Intelligence (MASINT):** Analyzing physical characteristics of targets, such as radar emissions or acoustic signatures.

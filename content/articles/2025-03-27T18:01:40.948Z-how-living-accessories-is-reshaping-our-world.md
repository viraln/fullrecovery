Here are some tips for maximizing space with small living room accessories:

1.  **Vertical Storage:** Utilize wall space with shelves, floating cabinets, and vertical organizers to maximize storage without taking up valuable floor space.
2.  **Mirrors:** Strategically placed mirrors can create the illusion of more space and reflect light, making the room feel brighter and airier.

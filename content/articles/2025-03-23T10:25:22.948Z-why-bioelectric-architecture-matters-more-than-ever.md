## Key Components of Bioelectric Building Materials

The foundation of bioelectric architecture lies in innovative building materials. These aren't just sustainable; they're actively involved in the building's performance:

* **Microbial Concrete:**  This material uses bacteria to self-heal cracks and enhance durability.  The bacteria consume calcium lactate, producing calcite (a key component of limestone) that fills cracks, extending the lifespan of the structure.
* **Bio-based Polymers:** Derived from renewable sources like plants, these polymers offer a sustainable alternative to traditional plastics and composites, reducing reliance on fossil fuels.
* **Living Walls and Green Roofs:**  These aren't just aesthetically pleasing; they improve air quality, regulate temperature, and reduce stormwater runoff.  ![An example of a building with a thriving living wall.](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1488972685288-c3fd157d7c7a?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress)

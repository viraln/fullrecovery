## Common Challenges and Solutions in Autonomous Design

While the potential is immense, challenges remain:

* **Data Bias:** AI models are trained on data, and biases in that data can lead to biased design outcomes.  Careful data curation and algorithm design are crucial to mitigate this.
* **Lack of Explainability:**  Understanding *why* an AI generated a particular design can be difficult.  Research into explainable AI is essential to build trust and transparency.
* **Ethical Considerations:**  The use of AI in design raises ethical questions about intellectual property, job displacement, and the potential for misuse.  Careful consideration of these issues is vital.

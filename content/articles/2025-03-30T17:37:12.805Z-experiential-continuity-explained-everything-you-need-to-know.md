## What is Experiential Continuity?

Experiential continuity refers to the degree to which a customer's interactions across all touchpoints and channels of a brand feel unified, consistent, and relevant. It's about creating a *seamless customer experience* that eliminates friction, anticipates needs, and reinforces brand identity. Think of it as a single, ongoing conversation with your customer, regardless of how or where they choose to engage with you.

Unlike simple *consistent brand experience*, which focuses on maintaining a similar look, feel, and messaging across channels, experiential continuity goes further. It's about remembering past interactions, understanding the customer's current context, and proactively tailoring future interactions to their specific needs and preferences. It's about creating a cohesive *omnichannel customer journey* that feels natural and intuitive.

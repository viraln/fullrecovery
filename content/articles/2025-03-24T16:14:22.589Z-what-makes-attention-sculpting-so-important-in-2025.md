## Attention Sculpting Techniques: A Practical Guide

**2. Open Monitoring Meditation:** This involves paying attention to whatever arises in your experience without judgment. It enhances your awareness of distractions and helps you develop a more flexible and adaptable attentional style.

**3. Attention Shifting Exercises:** These involve deliberately shifting your focus between different stimuli, training your brain to switch gears efficiently. Examples include alternating between focusing on a visual image and an auditory sound.

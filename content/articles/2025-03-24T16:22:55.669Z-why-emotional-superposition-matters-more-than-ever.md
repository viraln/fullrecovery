## What is Emotional Superposition?

Emotional superposition, a concept drawing from quantum physics, describes the state of experiencing multiple, often conflicting, emotions simultaneously.  Unlike feeling sad *or* happy, it's the experience of feeling both sad *and* happy, anxious *and* excited, all at once.  This isn't simply mood swings; it's a sustained state of internal dissonance. ![A graphic depicting overlapping emotional states, such as joy and sadness](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1451187580459-43490279c0fa?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress). The psychological underpinnings of emotional superposition are multifaceted.

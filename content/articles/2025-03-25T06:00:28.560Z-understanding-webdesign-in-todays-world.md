## Latest Trends in Web Design (2025)

The world of web design is constantly evolving. Some of the hottest trends in 2025 include:

* **AI-powered design tools:**  These tools are automating many design tasks, making the design process faster and more efficient.
* **Increased focus on accessibility:**  Web designers are increasingly prioritizing accessibility for users with disabilities.
* **Immersive experiences:**  Virtual reality (VR) and augmented reality (AR) are being integrated into websites to create more engaging experiences.
* **Sustainable web design:**  Designers are increasingly focusing on the environmental impact of websites, using eco-friendly hosting and design practices.

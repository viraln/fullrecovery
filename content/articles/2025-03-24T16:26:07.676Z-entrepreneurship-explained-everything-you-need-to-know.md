## 5. Funding for Startups in 2025: Navigating the Funding Landscape

Securing funding is a crucial aspect of starting a business.  Several options exist:

* **Bootstrapping:** Self-funding your business using personal savings or revenue.
* **Small Business Loans:**  Securing loans from banks or credit unions.
* **Angel Investors:**  Seeking funding from individual investors who provide capital in exchange for equity.
* **Venture Capital:**  Attracting investment from firms that specialize in funding high-growth startups.
* **Crowdfunding:**  Raising funds from a large number of individuals through online platforms.

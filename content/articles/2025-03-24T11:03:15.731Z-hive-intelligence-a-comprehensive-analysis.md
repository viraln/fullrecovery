## Understanding Hive Intelligence and Swarm Intelligence

Hive intelligence, often used interchangeably with swarm intelligence, refers to the collective behavior of decentralized, self-organizing systems.  It's not about a single, central brain controlling the group, but rather about individual agents interacting locally, leading to global emergent behavior.  Think of it as the wisdom of the crowds, amplified exponentially.  The "hive mind definition," often misinterpreted as a monolithic consciousness, is actually a complex interplay of simple rules and interactions.

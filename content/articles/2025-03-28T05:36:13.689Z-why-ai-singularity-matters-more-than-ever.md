## Understanding the AI Singularity: A Core Definition

At its heart, the AI Singularity, also known as the Technological Singularity or Artificial Intelligence Singularity, represents a hypothetical point in time when artificial intelligence surpasses human intelligence. This isn't just about AI becoming smarter in narrow tasks; it's about AI achieving general intelligence (AGI) – the ability to understand, learn, and apply knowledge across a wide range of domains, much like a human. More crucially, it involves AI becoming capable of self-improvement, recursively enhancing its own capabilities at an exponential rate. This runaway effect is what defines the "singularity" – a point beyond which prediction becomes virtually impossible.

> **EXPERT TIP:** The key element of the Singularity isn't simply AI surpassing human intelligence, but AI's ability to *improve itself* without human intervention. This creates a feedback loop of exponential growth.

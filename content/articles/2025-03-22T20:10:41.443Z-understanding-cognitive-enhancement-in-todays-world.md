## Cognitive Enhancement Drugs: Prescription Options

For individuals with diagnosed cognitive impairments like ADHD or Alzheimer's disease, prescription medications can play a vital role in cognitive enhancement.  These drugs should only be used under strict medical supervision.  Examples include stimulants for ADHD and cholinesterase inhibitors for Alzheimer's.  The use of these drugs for cognitive enhancement in healthy individuals is generally not recommended and carries potential risks.

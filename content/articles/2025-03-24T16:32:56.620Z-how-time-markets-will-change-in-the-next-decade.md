## What Are Time Markets?

Time markets, in their simplest form, represent the trading of the value of time itself.  This isn't about literal time travel, but rather the prediction and exploitation of temporal price fluctuations.  Think of it this way:  the price of an asset at 9:00 AM might be different than at 3:00 PM.  Time markets are built around capitalizing on these intraday, intraweak, or even inter-annual variations. ## Time Market Trading: Strategies and Approaches

Several strategies are emerging within time markets.

## Femtotech vs. Picosecond Laser: Understanding the Differences

While both femtosecond and picosecond lasers are ultra-short pulsed lasers, their pulse durations significantly impact their applications.  Femtosecond lasers, with pulses in the range of hundreds of femtoseconds, offer superior precision and minimal heat-affected zones.  Picosecond lasers, with pulse durations in the picosecond range (trillionths of a second), are less precise but often more cost-effective.

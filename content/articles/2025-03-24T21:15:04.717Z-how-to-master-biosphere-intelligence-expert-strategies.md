## What is Biosphere Intelligence?

Biosphere Intelligence (BI) represents the intersection of artificial intelligence (AI) and closed ecological systems.  It involves the development of intelligent systems that interact with and learn from dynamic, self-regulating environments, like miniature biospheres.  These systems use AI algorithms to monitor, analyze, and even control various aspects of the biosphere, optimizing resource management, predicting outcomes, and ultimately improving the overall health and sustainability of the system.

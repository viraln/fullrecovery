## The Allure of Computational Democracy: Benefits and Opportunities

## Navigating the Risks: Security and Ethical Considerations

While the potential benefits are significant, the risks associated with computational democracy must be carefully addressed.  The most pressing concern is security.  Online voting systems are vulnerable to hacking, manipulation, and denial-of-service attacks.  Ensuring the integrity and security of these systems is paramount. Equally important are ethical considerations.  Algorithmic bias, data privacy concerns, and the digital divide must be proactively addressed to prevent exacerbating existing inequalities.  ![A graphic illustrating potential security threats to online voting systems](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1540908489236-15aac66d9a53?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress)

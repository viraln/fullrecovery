## Iterative and Recursive Computing: A Deeper Dive

It's crucial to differentiate between iterative and recursive computing, although they are often used interchangeably in the context of self-similar algorithms.  Iterative computing involves repeating a set of instructions until a condition is met. Recursive computing, on the other hand, involves a function calling itself.

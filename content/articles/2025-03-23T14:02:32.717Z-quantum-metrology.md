## Challenges and Solutions in Quantum Metrology

Despite its enormous potential, quantum metrology faces several challenges:

* **Environmental noise:**  Quantum sensors are highly sensitive to environmental noise, which can degrade their performance.  Sophisticated noise-reduction techniques are crucial.
* **Cost:** Currently, many quantum sensors are expensive to manufacture and operate, limiting their widespread adoption.

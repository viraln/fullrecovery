## What Are Meaning Markets?  Understanding the Core Principles

Meaning Markets represent a significant shift in the business paradigm.  They move beyond the traditional focus on pure profit maximization, instead emphasizing the creation of value for all stakeholders – customers, employees, communities, and the environment.  These markets prioritize purpose, sustainability, and social impact alongside financial success.  ![A diverse group of people collaborating on a project](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1620969427101-7a2bb6d83273?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress)

## Understanding Subjective Time: More Than Just a Feeling

Subjective time, unlike objective time measured by clocks, is the personal experience of time's duration. It's shaped by a complex interplay of factors:  attention, emotional arousal, memory encoding, and the perceived novelty of an experience.

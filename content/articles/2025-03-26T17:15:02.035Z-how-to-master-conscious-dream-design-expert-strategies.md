## Deepening Your Lucid Dream Experience

Once you achieve lucidity, maintaining it can be challenging. These techniques can help stabilize and deepen your dream control:

1. **Spin Technique:** If your dream starts to fade, spin your dream body. This often re-establishes the dream's vividness.
2. **Sensory Intensification:** Focus on your dream senses. Touch objects, listen to sounds, and truly experience the dream environment. This reinforces the dream state.
3. **Dream Yoga:** Practice specific movements and visualizations within the dream to enhance clarity and control.

> **EXPERT TIP:** Keep a dream journal by your bed to record your dreams immediately upon waking. This strengthens dream recall and helps identify recurring dream signs.

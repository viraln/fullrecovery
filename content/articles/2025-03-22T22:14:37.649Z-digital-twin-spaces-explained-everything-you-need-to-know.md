## What are Digital Twin Spaces?

Digital twin spaces are virtual representations of physical spaces, objects, or systems.  They leverage advanced technologies like IoT, AI, machine learning, and 3D modeling to create highly accurate, dynamic simulations. Unlike simple 3D models, digital twin spaces are constantly updated with real-time data from their physical counterparts, providing a continuously evolving and incredibly insightful reflection of the real world.

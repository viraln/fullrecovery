## What is a Digital Twin?

A digital twin is a virtual representation of a physical object, process, or system. It's a dynamic, data-driven model that continuously updates itself based on real-time data collected from its physical counterpart. This data allows the digital twin to mirror the performance, behavior, and condition of the original, providing valuable insights for optimization, predictive maintenance, and improved decision-making.  Think of it as a sophisticated simulation constantly learning and adapting. ![A graphic illustrating the connection between a physical object and its digital twin](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1592084284963-884b5d66f7cc?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress)

> **EXPERT TIP:**  The accuracy and effectiveness of a digital twin are directly proportional to the quality and quantity of data it receives.

## Common Challenges and Solutions in 6G Development

The path to 6G isn't without its hurdles:

* **High Frequency Challenges:**  Terahertz waves are easily absorbed by atmospheric elements, limiting range and requiring more frequent base stations.  Solutions involve developing advanced materials and antenna designs.
* **Power Consumption:**  6G devices will require efficient power management to avoid short battery life.  Research is focusing on energy-efficient hardware and software.
* **Standardization:**  Reaching global consensus on 6G standards is crucial for interoperability.  International collaborations are vital for achieving this goal.

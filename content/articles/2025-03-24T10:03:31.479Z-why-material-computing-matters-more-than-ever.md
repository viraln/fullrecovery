## What is Material Computing?

Material computing transcends the limitations of traditional silicon-based computing by leveraging the inherent computational properties of various materials.  Instead of relying solely on electrons flowing through transistors, material computing harnesses phenomena like chemical reactions, phase transitions, or even the mechanical properties of materials to perform computations.

## Understanding the Closed-Loop Ecosystem: A Self-Sustaining Symphony

A closed-loop habitat, also known as a closed-loop ecosystem or bioregenerative life support system, is an environment designed to mimic the self-sufficiency of natural ecosystems.  Unlike open systems that rely on external inputs, closed-loop habitats strive for complete resource recycling.  Waste is repurposed, energy is conserved, and the system strives for a state of dynamic equilibrium. This means air, water, and waste are continuously recycled, minimizing reliance on external resources. ![A diagram illustrating the cyclical nature of resource flow within a closed-loop habitat](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1564756631354-009c12675629?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress)

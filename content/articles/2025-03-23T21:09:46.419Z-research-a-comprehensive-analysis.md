## Choosing the Right Research Methods: Qualitative vs. Quantitative

The choice between qualitative and quantitative research methods depends heavily on your research question and objectives.  Each approach offers unique strengths and weaknesses:

| Feature          | Qualitative Research Methods                               | Quantitative Research Methods                             |
|-----------------|-----------------------------------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------|
| **Data Type**    | Text, images, audio, video                               | Numbers, statistics                                      |
| **Approach**     | Exploratory, in-depth understanding                      | Measurement, statistical analysis                         |
| **Sample Size**  | Smaller, focused samples                                  | Larger, representative samples                           |
| **Data Analysis**| Interpretation, thematic analysis, narrative analysis       | Statistical tests, data modeling                         |
| **Examples**     | Interviews, focus groups, case studies, ethnography       | Surveys, experiments, statistical analysis of existing data |

> **DID YOU KNOW?** Mixed methods research, combining both qualitative and quantitative approaches, is increasingly popular, offering a more holistic understanding of complex phenomena.

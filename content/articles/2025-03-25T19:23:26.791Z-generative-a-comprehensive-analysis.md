## Common Challenges and Solutions

Despite its potential, generative AI faces several challenges:

* **Bias and Fairness:** Generative models can inherit biases from their training data, leading to unfair or discriminatory outputs.  Addressing this requires careful curation of training datasets and the development of bias mitigation techniques.
* **Computational Cost:** Training and deploying generative models can be computationally expensive, requiring significant resources.  Optimizations and specialized hardware are essential to mitigate this.
* **Lack of Control:**  Fine-tuning the output of generative models can be challenging, requiring significant expertise and iterative experimentation.
* **Intellectual Property Concerns:**  The legal landscape surrounding AI-generated content is still evolving, raising questions about ownership and copyright.

## What is Multispecies Justice?

Multispecies justice is a philosophical and practical framework that challenges the human-centric worldview that has dominated Western thought and action for centuries. It argues that justice isn't solely a human concern, but extends to all living beings and the ecosystems they inhabit.  It moves beyond simply acknowledging the intrinsic value of non-human life to actively working towards dismantling systems of oppression that harm them.

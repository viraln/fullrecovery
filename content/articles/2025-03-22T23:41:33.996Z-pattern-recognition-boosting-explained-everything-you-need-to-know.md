## Common Challenges and Their Solutions

Despite its power, pattern recognition faces challenges:

* **Data Quality:**  Poor quality data can lead to inaccurate results.  Data cleaning and preprocessing are crucial steps.

* **Overfitting:**  ML models can sometimes overfit the training data, performing well on the training set but poorly on unseen data.  Techniques like cross-validation can mitigate this.

* **Computational Cost:**  Processing large datasets can be computationally expensive, requiring significant computing resources.

* **Interpretability:**  Understanding the reasons behind the predictions of complex ML models can be challenging.  XAI techniques aim to address this.

## Algorithmic Transparency:  Opening the Black Box and Building Trust

Algorithmic transparency is crucial for accountability and public trust.  It involves making the inner workings of algorithms understandable to both experts and non-experts.  This doesn't necessarily mean revealing the entire source code, but rather providing clear explanations of the algorithm's logic, data sources, and potential impacts.

**Challenges to Algorithmic Transparency:**

*   Trade secrets protection.
*   Complexity of algorithms.
*   Lack of standardized metrics for evaluating transparency.

**Solutions:**

*   Developing standardized reporting frameworks.
*   Creating explainable AI (XAI) techniques.
*   Promoting open-source development for certain applications.

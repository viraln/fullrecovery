## Global Workspace Theory (GWT): Broadcasting Consciousness

GWT posits that consciousness arises from a "global workspace" ‚Äì a central system that broadcasts information across various specialized brain modules.  This allows for integrated processing and the creation of a unified conscious experience. ## Integrated Information Theory (IIT): Maximizing Information Integration

IIT, on the other hand, focuses on the *amount* of integrated information within a system. ## Neural Correlates of Consciousness (NCC):  Pinpointing the Network Activity

NCC research aims to identify the minimal neural mechanisms sufficient for specific conscious experiences.  This involves using neuroimaging techniques like fMRI and EEG to pinpoint the brain regions and activity patterns associated with different conscious states.  While pinpointing specific regions is difficult, studies consistently demonstrate widespread network activity during conscious experiences.

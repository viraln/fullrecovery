## Collaborative Robots (Cobots): The Future of Human-Robot Interaction

Collaborative robots, or cobots, represent a significant shift in robotics. Unlike traditional industrial robots that operate in isolation, cobots are designed to work safely alongside humans. They are smaller, lighter, and often equipped with sensors to prevent collisions.  This allows for more flexible and efficient manufacturing processes. ![A human working alongside a collaborative robot in a factory setting](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1563968743333-044cef800494?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress)

**Pros:**
* Increased productivity through human-robot collaboration.
* Enhanced workplace safety due to inherent safety features.
* Flexibility to adapt to changing production needs.

**Cons:**
* Higher initial investment compared to traditional robots.
* Requires careful safety protocols and training.
* Limited payload capacity compared to industrial robots.

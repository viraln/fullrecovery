##  Microbiome Supplements: A Supporting Role

While diet forms the cornerstone of microbiome design, targeted microbiome supplements can provide additional support.  However, choosing the *best microbiome supplements* requires careful consideration.  Not all supplements are created equal, and some may even contain inactive or poorly researched strains.

**Types of Microbiome Supplements:**

* **Probiotic Supplements:**  Choose high-quality supplements with well-researched strains and guaranteed viability.
* **Prebiotic Supplements:**  Look for supplements containing a variety of fibers to support diverse bacterial communities.
* **Postbiotics:**  These are metabolites produced by beneficial bacteria and may offer various health benefits.

> **DID YOU KNOW?**  The efficacy of probiotic supplements can vary significantly depending on factors like strain, dosage, and individual gut environment.

## Understanding Cognitive Meta-Processes: The "Thinking About Thinking" Revolution

Cognitive meta-processes, often shortened to "metacognition," are the higher-level cognitive functions that allow us to monitor, regulate, and control our own thinking processes.  It's essentially "thinking about thinking."  This involves self-reflection on our strategies, understanding our strengths and weaknesses, and adapting our approaches accordingly.  Think of it as your brain's internal supervisor, overseeing and optimizing its own operations.  ![Brain with highlighted areas representing metacognitive processes](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1585808195113-99cefc8de6ef?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress)

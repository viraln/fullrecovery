## The Many-Worlds Interpretation: A Quick Primer

Before diving into the ethical complexities, let's briefly revisit the Many-Worlds Interpretation.  Unlike the Copenhagen interpretation, which suggests wave function collapse, MWI postulates that every quantum event creates a branching of universes.  ![A visual representation of branching universes](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1487412840181-f63f62e6a0ee?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress).

## Common Challenges and Solutions

Building and maintaining space habitats presents numerous challenges:

* **Radiation:**  Shielding against harmful radiation is crucial.  Solutions include using thick shielding materials, locating habitats underground, or using magnetic fields.
* **Resource Management:**  Efficient resource utilization and recycling are critical for long-term sustainability. Closed-loop life support systems and in-situ resource utilization (ISRU) are key solutions.
* **Psychological Factors:**  The psychological impact of prolonged isolation and confinement must be addressed through careful crew selection, habitat design, and communication strategies.

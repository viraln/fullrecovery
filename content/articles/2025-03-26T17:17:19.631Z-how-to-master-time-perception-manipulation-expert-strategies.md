## Factors Influencing Your Perception of Time

Several factors can significantly impact how we experience time. Understanding these influences is crucial for effective time perception manipulation.

*   **Emotion:** Strong emotions, both positive and negative, can distort our perception of time. Fear, for example, can make time seem to slow down, while joy can make it fly by.
*   **Attention:** When we're deeply focused on a task, time often seems to disappear. Conversely, when we're bored or distracted, time drags on.
*   **Novelty:** Novel experiences create more memories, making time seem longer in retrospect. Familiar routines, on the other hand, compress our sense of time.
*   **Age:** As we age, our perception of time can change. The "time flies" phenomenon is often attributed to the decrease in novel experiences as we get older.
*   **Physical State:** Fatigue, stress, and even hunger can alter our sense of time.

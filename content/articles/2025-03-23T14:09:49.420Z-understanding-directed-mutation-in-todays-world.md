## What is Directed Mutation? A Deep Dive into the Process

Directed mutation, also known as directed evolution or adaptive mutation, is a powerful technique used to engineer desirable traits in organisms. Unlike random mutagenesis, where mutations occur haphazardly, directed mutation employs targeted strategies to increase the frequency of beneficial mutations.  This involves multiple rounds of mutagenesis followed by selection for the desired phenotype.  The process essentially accelerates evolution in a laboratory setting, guiding organisms toward specific goals.  ![A graphic illustrating the steps involved in directed mutation](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1643780668909-580822430155?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress).

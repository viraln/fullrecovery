##  Common Challenges and Solutions in Space-Native Design

Developing space-native applications presents unique challenges:

* **Motion Sickness:**  Poorly designed interfaces can induce motion sickness in users.  Solutions include minimizing jarring movements and providing options for adjusting the field of view.
* **Cognitive Overload:**  Too much information or complex interactions can overwhelm users.  Solutions include employing clear visual hierarchies, intuitive navigation, and minimalist design principles.
* **Accessibility:**  Ensuring accessibility for users with disabilities is crucial.  Solutions include providing alternative input methods, auditory cues, and customizable interface options.
* **Technical Limitations:**  Current technology has limitations in terms of processing power, rendering capabilities, and bandwidth.  Solutions include optimizing applications for performance and utilizing cloud-based services.

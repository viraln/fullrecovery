## Measuring Value Generation: Key Metrics

Effectively measuring value generation requires a comprehensive approach.  While traditional metrics like revenue and profit remain important, they don't tell the whole story.  Consider these additional metrics:

* **Customer Satisfaction (CSAT):** Measures how satisfied customers are with your products or services.
* **Net Promoter Score (NPS):** Gauges customer loyalty and willingness to recommend your brand.
* **Customer Lifetime Value (CLTV):** Estimates the total revenue generated by a single customer over their relationship with your company.
* **Employee Satisfaction:**  Happy employees are more productive and contribute to better value generation.
* **Environmental Impact:**  Measuring your carbon footprint and other sustainability metrics.

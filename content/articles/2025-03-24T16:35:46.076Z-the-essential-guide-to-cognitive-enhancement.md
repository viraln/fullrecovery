## Cognitive Enhancement Drugs: Prescription Options

In certain cases, prescription medications may be used to treat cognitive impairments associated with specific conditions like Alzheimer's disease or ADHD. These drugs should only be prescribed and monitored by a qualified medical professional.  Examples include donepezil (for Alzheimer's) and methylphenidate (for ADHD).  Self-medicating with these drugs is extremely dangerous and can have serious health consequences.

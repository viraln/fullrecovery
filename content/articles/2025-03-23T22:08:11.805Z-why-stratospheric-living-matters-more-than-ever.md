## Stratospheric Habitat Design: Engineering the Impossible

Creating permanent habitats in the stratosphere presents significant engineering challenges.  These include:

* **Radiation Shielding:**  The stratosphere receives significantly more radiation than the Earth's surface, necessitating robust shielding.
* **Pressure Regulation:**  Maintaining a breathable atmosphere within a pressurized habitat is crucial.
* **Life Support Systems:**  Providing a sustainable supply of air, water, food, and waste management is essential for long-term habitation.
* **Material Selection:**  Materials must withstand extreme temperature fluctuations, radiation, and atmospheric pressure.

## Crafting Your Best Workout Routine

Designing the perfect workout routine depends heavily on your goals and fitness level.  A balanced program typically incorporates cardio, strength training, and flexibility exercises.

**For Weight Loss (Weight Loss Workout):**  A combination of high-intensity interval training (HIIT) and moderate-intensity cardio, coupled with strength training 2-3 times a week, is highly effective.

**For Muscle Gain (Build Muscle at Home):** Focus on strength training exercises targeting all major muscle groups, incorporating progressive overload (gradually increasing weight or resistance).  Include adequate rest and recovery.

**Sample Weekly Routine (Beginner):**

* **Monday:** Strength Training (Upper Body)
* **Tuesday:** Cardio (30 minutes)
* **Wednesday:** Rest or Active Recovery (yoga, stretching)
* **Thursday:** Strength Training (Lower Body)
* **Friday:** Cardio (30 minutes)
* **Saturday:** Rest or Active Recovery
* **Sunday:**  Flexibility & Mobility (yoga, Pilates)

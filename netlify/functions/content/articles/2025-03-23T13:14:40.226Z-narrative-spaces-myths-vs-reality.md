## What Are Narrative Spaces?  Unveiling the Essence

Narrative spaces, simply put, are environments designed to actively participate in storytelling. They're not just backdrops; they're integral characters in the narrative, influencing player/audience emotion, behavior, and understanding.  Unlike traditional storytelling, which relies primarily on text or dialogue, narrative spaces leverage the power of physical or virtual environments to convey meaning and drive the plot forward. Think of the difference between reading about a haunted house and *experiencing* one through a virtual reality game. That's the power of a well-designed narrative space.

> **EXPERT TIP:**  The key to a successful narrative space lies in seamless integration. The environment should subtly guide the narrative, enhancing the story without overwhelming it.

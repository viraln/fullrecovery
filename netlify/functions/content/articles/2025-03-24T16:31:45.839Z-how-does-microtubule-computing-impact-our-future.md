## Understanding Microtubule Computing: The Basics

Microtubules are protein structures found within all eukaryotic cells, playing crucial roles in cell division, intracellular transport, and maintaining cell shape.  ![Microtubule structure diagram](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1451187580459-43490279c0fa?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress).

## What is Teleological Engineering?

Teleological engineering, also known as teleological design or purpose-driven engineering, is a revolutionary approach to engineering that focuses on designing systems with pre-defined goals or purposes in mind. Unlike traditional engineering, which often reacts to problems, teleological engineering proactively designs systems to achieve specific, often complex, objectives.  It's about building things *for* a purpose, rather than simply *with* a purpose. This shift in perspective necessitates a deeper understanding of the desired outcomes and a more holistic approach to design and implementation.

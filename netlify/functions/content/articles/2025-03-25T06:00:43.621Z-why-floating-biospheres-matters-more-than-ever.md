## Understanding the Concept of a Floating Biosphere

A floating biosphere is essentially a closed or semi-closed ecological system designed to mimic the natural cycles of a terrestrial or aquatic environment.  Unlike Biosphere 2, a landmark project of the late 20th century, modern iterations aim for greater efficiency and sustainability. These structures, ranging from small-scale research units to potentially large-scale habitable environments, utilize advanced technologies to create a self-regulated environment capable of supporting life.

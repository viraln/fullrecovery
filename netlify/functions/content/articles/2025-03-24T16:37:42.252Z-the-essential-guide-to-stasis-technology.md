## Stasis Technology vs. Cryosleep: Separating Fact from Fiction

While often used interchangeably, stasis technology and cryosleep are distinct concepts.  Cryosleep, as depicted in science fiction, typically involves freezing the body to extremely low temperatures. Current research focuses on less extreme methods, such as induced hypothermia and pharmacological interventions, to slow down metabolic processes without the damaging effects of freezing.

* **Data Security:**  Protecting sensitive data is crucial.  **Solution:** Utilize secure form builders with robust encryption and security measures.
* **Integration Complexity:**  Integrating forms with other systems can be challenging.  **Solution:** Choose a form builder with robust integration capabilities and seek expert assistance if needed.

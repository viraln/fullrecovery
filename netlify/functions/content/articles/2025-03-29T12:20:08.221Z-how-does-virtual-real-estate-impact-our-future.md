## Understanding the Foundations: What is Virtual Real Estate?

Virtual real estate, at its simplest, refers to parcels of digital land existing within various metaverse platforms. These platforms, ranging from established names like Decentraland and The Sandbox to newer, emerging spaces, offer users the ability to buy, sell, develop, and monetize these virtual plots. Unlike physical real estate, virtual land is typically represented as a non-fungible token (NFT), ensuring unique ownership and traceability on the blockchain.

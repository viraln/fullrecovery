## The Impact of Cognitive Load Theory

Cognitive Load Theory (CLT) provides a valuable framework for understanding mental resource allocation. CLT posits that our working memory has a limited capacity, and exceeding this limit leads to cognitive overload. Understanding CLT helps us design strategies to manage the cognitive load of different tasks, breaking down complex projects into smaller, more manageable chunks.

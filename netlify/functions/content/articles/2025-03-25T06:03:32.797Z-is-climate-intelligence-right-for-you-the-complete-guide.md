## What is Climate Intelligence (CI)?

Climate intelligence, also known as climate change intelligence or climate risk intelligence, is the process of collecting, analyzing, and interpreting climate-related data to understand and mitigate climate risks.  It goes beyond simply acknowledging climate change; it involves proactively assessing its potential impact on your specific operations, assets, and stakeholders.  CI leverages advanced technologies like artificial intelligence (AI), machine learning (ML), and big data analytics to transform raw climate data into actionable insights.

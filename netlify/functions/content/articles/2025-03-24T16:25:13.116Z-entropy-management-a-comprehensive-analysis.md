## What is Entropy Management?

Entropy, a concept borrowed from thermodynamics, describes the natural tendency of systems to move from order to disorder. Entropy management, therefore, is the proactive process of identifying, analyzing, and mitigating these sources of disorder to maintain a state of optimal efficiency and productivity.

## 2. How Does Quartz Quantum Computing Work?

Quartz quantum computing leverages the unique properties of specific defects within the quartz crystal structure. **Step 1:**  Creating the Qubits:  Researchers use techniques like ion implantation or irradiation to introduce precisely controlled defects into the quartz lattice.

**Step 3:**  Entangling the Qubits:  Entanglement, a crucial aspect of quantum computing, allows for the creation of correlations between qubits, enabling complex computations. This is achieved through carefully designed interactions between the defects within the quartz crystal.

**Step 4:**  Measurement and Readout:  The quantum state of the qubits is measured using techniques such as fluorescence detection, providing the results of the quantum computation.

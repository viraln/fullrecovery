## What is Haptics?  A Deep Dive into the Science of Touch

Haptics, simply put, is the science and technology of touch. It involves creating and manipulating tactile sensations, allowing us to "feel" digital information. This isn't limited to simple vibrations; it encompasses a vast range of sensations, from subtle textures to powerful impacts.  The technology behind it utilizes various methods, including electro-vibration, ultrasound, and even magnetic fields, to stimulate our sense of touch.

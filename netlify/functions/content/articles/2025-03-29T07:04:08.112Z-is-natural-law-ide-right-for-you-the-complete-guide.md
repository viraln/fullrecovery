## What Exactly is Natural Law Programming?

Before diving into the IDE, it's crucial to grasp the underlying philosophy of Natural Law programming. This paradigm seeks to express code in a way that reflects the inherent principles governing the natural world. Think of it as moving away from the rigid, step-by-step instructions of traditional programming towards a more declarative, relationship-based approach.

Unlike imperative programming, where you explicitly tell the computer *how* to do something, Natural Law programming focuses on *what* needs to be achieved. The system then infers the optimal execution path based on pre-defined rules and relationships. This often leads to more concise, understandable, and maintainable code.

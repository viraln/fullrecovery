While a comprehensive Matter command list is technically extensive and primarily for developers, understanding the *types* of commands available is crucial for understanding Matter's impact. Here's a simplified overview:

*   **On/Off Commands:** These are the most basic, controlling the power state of devices like lights, switches, and outlets. Example: "Turn on the living room lights."
*   **Level Control Commands:** Adjust brightness, volume, or fan speed. Example: "Dim the bedroom lights to 50%."
*   **Color Control Commands:** Change the color of smart bulbs or LED strips. Example: "Set the kitchen lights to blue."
*   **Thermostat Commands:** Adjust temperature, mode (heat, cool, auto), and fan speed. Example: "Set the thermostat to 72 degrees."
*   **Lock/Unlock Commands:** Control smart locks. Example: "Lock the front door."
*   **Scene Commands:** Activate pre-defined settings for multiple devices. Example: "Activate movie night scene."
*   **Identify Commands:** Trigger a device to blink or make a sound for identification purposes. Useful when adding new devices or troubleshooting.

## What are Aware Nanomaterials?

Aware nanomaterials, also known as smart nanomaterials, self-healing nanomaterials, or responsive nanomaterials, are engineered materials exhibiting a unique ability: they can sense changes in their surroundings and react autonomously.  This "awareness" stems from their inherent properties or embedded sensors, allowing them to adapt and modify their behavior in response to stimuli such as temperature, pH, light, or stress.

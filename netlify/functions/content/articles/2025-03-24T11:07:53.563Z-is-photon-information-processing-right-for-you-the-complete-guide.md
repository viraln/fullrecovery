## What is Photon Information Processing?

Photon information processing leverages the unique properties of photons â€“ particles of light â€“ to perform computations and transmit information. Unlike electrons in traditional computing, photons don't interact strongly with each other, minimizing interference and enabling highly parallel processing. This technology encompasses several areas, including:

* **Optical Computing:**  Using light instead of electricity for computation, offering massive parallel processing capabilities.
* **Quantum Computing Photonics:**  Harnessing the principles of quantum mechanics to perform calculations beyond the capabilities of classical computers.  This involves using photons as qubits, the fundamental units of quantum information.

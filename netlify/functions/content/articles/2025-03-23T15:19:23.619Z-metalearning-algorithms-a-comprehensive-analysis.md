## Key Concepts and Terminology

Understanding the fundamental concepts is crucial. Here are some key terms you'll encounter frequently:

* **Meta-learner:** The algorithm responsible for learning the meta-strategy.
* **Base-learner:** The algorithm used to solve individual tasks after being initialized by the meta-learner.
* **Meta-training:** The process of training the meta-learner on a diverse set of tasks.
* **Meta-testing:** Evaluating the meta-learner's ability to learn new, unseen tasks.
* **Meta-dataset:** A collection of datasets used for meta-training and meta-testing.

## Methodologies: Studying Alternate Timelines

How can you study something that may not even exist? That's the challenge Reality Branch Anthropology faces. Researchers employ a combination of methodologies, including:

*   **Counterfactual History:** Analyzing how specific historical events could have unfolded differently and the potential consequences.
*   **Thought Experiments:** Creating detailed scenarios of alternate realities based on specific "what if" questions.
*   **Extrapolation from Existing Data:** Using current anthropological knowledge to predict how societies might develop under different conditions.
*   **Computational Modeling:** Employing computer simulations to model the evolution of alternate societies.

✅ These methodologies are often combined to create a holistic and rigorous analysis of alternate realities.

## Singularity Forecasting Models

Several approaches attempt to model the singularity's timeline. These range from simple extrapolations of current technological trends to more sophisticated models that incorporate factors like Moore's Law, technological breakthroughs, and societal impacts.  

* **Extrapolation Models:** These models simply extend current trends into the future, assuming a constant rate of technological progress.  However, this approach often fails to account for unforeseen breakthroughs or limitations.
* **Agent-Based Modeling:** This more complex approach simulates the interactions of various actors (humans, AI systems, organizations) to predict the overall trajectory of technological development.
* **Technological Forecasting:** This involves expert panels and surveys to assess the likelihood and timing of specific technological advancements.

> **EXPERT TIP:**  No single model provides a definitive answer.  A combination of approaches offers a more nuanced and reliable prediction.

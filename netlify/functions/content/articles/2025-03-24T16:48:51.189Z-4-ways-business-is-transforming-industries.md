## 2. The Exponential Growth of the Sharing Economy

The sharing economy, fueled by platforms connecting individuals and businesses, continues its explosive growth. From ride-sharing and home-sharing to collaborative workspaces and peer-to-peer lending, this model is disrupting traditional industries.

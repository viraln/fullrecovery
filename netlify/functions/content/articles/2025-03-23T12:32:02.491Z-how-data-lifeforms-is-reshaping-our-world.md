## What Are Data Lifeforms?  Understanding the Basics

Data lifeforms, also known as digital lifeforms or synthetic lifeforms, are complex computational entities that exhibit characteristics traditionally associated with living organisms.  They aren't alive in the biological sense, lacking DNA and cellular structures.  Instead, they exist as intricate patterns of information, evolving and adapting within simulated environments.  These environments can be anything from a simple computer program to a vast, interconnected network.

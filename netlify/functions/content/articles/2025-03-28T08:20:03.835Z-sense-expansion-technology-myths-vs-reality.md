## What Exactly is Sense Expansion Technology?

Sense expansion technology encompasses a range of devices, techniques, and research aimed at enhancing or augmenting human sensory perception. It goes beyond simply correcting impairments like hearing aids or glasses. Instead, it focuses on expanding the *range* and *types* of sensory information we can process. This includes:

*   **Sensory Augmentation:** Adding new sensory inputs or enhancing existing ones. Think of a device that allows you to "feel" changes in air pressure or "see" the Wi-Fi signal strength.
*   **Sensory Substitution:** Replacing one sense with another. For example, using sound to represent visual information for the visually impaired.
*   **Sensory Restoration:** Restoring lost or impaired sensory function, such as cochlear implants for hearing loss or retinal prostheses for vision loss. (While this is related, this article focuses more on augmentation *beyond* normal human capabilities.)

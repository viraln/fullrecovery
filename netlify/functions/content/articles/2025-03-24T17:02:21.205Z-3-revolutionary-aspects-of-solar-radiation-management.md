## 1.  The Precision of Stratospheric Aerosol Injection (SAI): A Surgical Approach to Climate Control

Stratospheric Aerosol Injection (SAI) is arguably the most researched SRM technique. It involves injecting reflective aerosols, such as sulfate particles, into the stratosphere to mimic the cooling effect of large volcanic eruptions.  While seemingly simple, the precision required is revolutionary.

## 2. Marine Cloud Brightening (MCB): Harnessing Nature's Reflective Power

Marine Cloud Brightening (MCB) offers a fascinating alternative to SAI. This technique involves spraying seawater into low-lying marine clouds, increasing their reflectivity.  The process utilizes naturally occurring sea salt particles as cloud condensation nuclei, enhancing their ability to reflect sunlight.

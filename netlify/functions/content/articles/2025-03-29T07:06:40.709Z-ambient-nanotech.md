## The Science Behind Self-Assembling Nanostructures

A cornerstone of ambient nanotech is the concept of self-assembling nanostructures. These are nanoscale components designed to spontaneously organize themselves into functional structures without external intervention. This process is driven by intermolecular forces, such as van der Waals forces, electrostatic interactions, and hydrogen bonding.

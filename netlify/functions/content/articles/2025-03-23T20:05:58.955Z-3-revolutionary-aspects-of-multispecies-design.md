## What is Multispecies Design?

Multispecies design, at its core, is a design philosophy that prioritizes the needs and well-being of all species, not just humans. It moves beyond anthropocentric approaches, recognizing the interconnectedness of life and the vital role biodiversity plays in healthy ecosystems.  It's about designing spaces and systems that actively support and enhance the lives of plants, animals, fungi, and microorganisms alongside human inhabitants.  Instead of viewing nature as a resource to be exploited, multispecies design views it as a partner in the design process. ![A vibrant illustration showcasing diverse species interacting in a designed environment](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1476357471311-43c0db9fb2b4?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress).

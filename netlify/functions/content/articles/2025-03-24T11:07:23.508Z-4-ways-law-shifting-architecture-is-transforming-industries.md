## 1. Parametric Architecture: Unleashing Algorithmic Design

Parametric architecture, a cornerstone of law-shifting design, uses algorithms and computer programming to generate building forms.  Instead of relying on traditional drafting methods, architects define parameters (variables) that dictate the building's geometry, materials, and performance.  This allows for unprecedented levels of customization and optimization.  ![Example of a parametrically designed building with complex, flowing curves.](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1527576539890-dfa815648363?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress)

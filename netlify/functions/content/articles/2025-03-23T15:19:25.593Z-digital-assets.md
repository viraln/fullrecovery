## Digital Asset Management (DAM)

Effective digital asset management is crucial for maximizing value and minimizing risks.  DAM involves organizing, storing, retrieving, and distributing digital assets efficiently and securely.  A robust DAM strategy includes:

1. **Centralized Storage:**  Consolidate your assets in a secure, centralized repository.
2. **Metadata Tagging:**  Use descriptive metadata to easily find and categorize assets.
3. **Access Control:**  Implement robust access control measures to protect sensitive assets.
4. **Version Control:**  Track changes and revisions to ensure you're using the most up-to-date versions.
5. **Workflow Automation:** Streamline processes for asset creation, approval, and distribution.

> **Did you know?**  Poor DAM practices can lead to significant losses due to duplicated efforts, lost files, and legal issues.

* **Light pollution:** This significantly hinders observation in urban areas.  Solutions include traveling to darker locations or using light pollution filters.
* **Weather conditions:** Cloudy skies and atmospheric turbulence can affect observations.  Patience and weather forecasting are crucial.
* **Cost of equipment:** Astronomy equipment can be expensive.  Start with basic equipment and upgrade gradually.

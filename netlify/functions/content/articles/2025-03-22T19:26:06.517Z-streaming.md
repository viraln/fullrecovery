## How to Cancel Streaming Subscriptions (and Why You Should)

Subscription fatigue is real.  It's easy to accumulate numerous streaming services without realizing the total cost.  Here's how to cancel:

1. **Step 1:** Log in to your account on the streaming service's website.
2. **Step 2:** Navigate to your account settings.
3. **Step 3:** Locate the "Subscription" or "Membership" section.
4. **Step 4:** Follow the instructions to cancel your subscription.  This might involve confirming your decision.
5. **Step 5:** Note the cancellation date and any associated charges.

> **EXPERT TIP:**  Regularly review your streaming subscriptions.  Cancel services you're not actively using to avoid unnecessary expenses.  This is especially important if you're using a free trial.  Remember to cancel BEFORE the trial ends to avoid automatic charges.

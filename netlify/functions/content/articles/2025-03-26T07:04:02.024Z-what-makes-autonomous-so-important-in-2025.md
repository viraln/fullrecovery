##  Autonomous Car Safety Features: A Deep Dive

The safety of autonomous vehicles is paramount. Advanced safety features are crucial for building public trust and ensuring safe deployment.  These include:

* **Advanced Driver-Assistance Systems (ADAS):**  Features like adaptive cruise control, lane keeping assist, and automatic emergency braking are foundational to autonomous driving.
* **Sensor Fusion:** Combining data from multiple sensors (LiDAR, radar, cameras) provides a comprehensive understanding of the vehicle's surroundings.
* **Redundancy Systems:** Multiple backups for critical systems ensure continued operation even if a primary component fails.
* **Cybersecurity Measures:** Protecting autonomous vehicles from hacking attempts is crucial for safety and security.

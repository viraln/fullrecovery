## Atomic Design vs. Modular Design: A Clear Distinction

While both approaches promote reusability, they differ significantly.  Modular design focuses on larger, more self-contained modules, often encompassing entire sections of a page. Atomic design, on the other hand, breaks down the interface into its smallest, most fundamental components.  Atomic design offers greater flexibility and scalability due to its granular nature.

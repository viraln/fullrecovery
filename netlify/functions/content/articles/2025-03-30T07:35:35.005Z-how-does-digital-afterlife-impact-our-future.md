## What Happens to Your Data After Death? Understanding the Legal Landscape

The legal landscape surrounding digital assets after death is still evolving. In many jurisdictions, digital assets are not automatically considered part of a deceased person's estate. This means that a digital executor may need specific legal authorization to access and manage online accounts.

> **DID YOU KNOW:** In 2016, the Uniform Law Commission approved the Revised Uniform Fiduciary Access to Digital Assets Act (RUFADAA), which provides a legal framework for granting fiduciaries access to a deceased person's digital assets. However, not all states have adopted RUFADAA.

## Studying Extraterrestrial Intelligence: Current Approaches and Limitations

Currently, the study of extraterrestrial intelligence relies on a combination of observational data and theoretical modeling. SETI programs scan the skies for radio signals or other signs of technology. *   **Radio Bias:** We primarily search for radio signals, assuming that aliens will use similar technology. This may be a false assumption.

*   **Anthropocentric Assumptions:** We tend to interpret potential signals through a human lens, which can lead to misinterpretations.

## 1.  Generating Killer Startup Ideas:  From Concept to Validation

The foundation of any successful startup is a compelling idea.  But not just *any* idea ‚Äì a *validated* one. **Step 1: Brainstorming & Ideation:**  Start by identifying problems you're passionate about solving.  Consider your own experiences, frustrations, and areas of expertise.

## What is Synthetic DNA?

Synthetic DNA, also known as artificial DNA, is DNA that is artificially manufactured in a laboratory rather than occurring naturally in living organisms.  It’s essentially a custom-designed sequence of nucleotides (adenine, guanine, cytosine, and thymine), assembled to create specific genetic constructs.  Unlike natural DNA extracted from organisms, synthetic DNA offers precise control over its sequence and length, opening a world of possibilities. ![Microscopic image of synthetic DNA strands](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1681127910416-f18e60b0ba1a?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress)

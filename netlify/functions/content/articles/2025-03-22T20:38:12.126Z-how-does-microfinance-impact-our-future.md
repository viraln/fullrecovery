## The Rise of Microfinance: From Grassroots to Global Impact

What began as small-scale initiatives has transformed into a global industry, reaching millions worldwide. ![A chart showing the growth of microfinance globally over the past 20 years](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1638544253786-ab3078fe2f3f?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress).

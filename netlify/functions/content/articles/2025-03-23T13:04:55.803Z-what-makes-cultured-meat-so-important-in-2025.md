## The Rise of Clean Meat: A Paradigm Shift in Food Production

Cultured meat, also known as lab-grown meat, clean meat, or cell-based meat, represents a seismic shift in how we produce animal protein.  Instead of raising and slaughtering animals, cultured meat is created by cultivating animal cells in a bioreactor.  These cells, typically taken from a small tissue sample, are nourished with a nutrient-rich media, allowing them to multiply and form muscle tissue, fat, and other components of meat.  ![Microscopic image of cultured meat cells growing in a bioreactor](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1675774644393-7bcfa424dec7?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress)

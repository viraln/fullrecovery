## Types of Magnetic Field Habitats and Their Design

Several magnetic field habitat designs are being explored, each with its own advantages and disadvantages.  These include:

* **Toroidal Habitats:**  These habitats utilize a ring-shaped structure with a powerful magnet at the center, generating a field that protects the entire interior. ![Diagram of a toroidal magnetic field habitat](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1559212958-2c88ccd83dde?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress)
* **Spherical Habitats:**  Spherical designs offer greater volume for a given surface area, potentially leading to more efficient resource management. However, generating a uniform field around a sphere presents significant engineering challenges.
* **Modular Habitats:**  These involve multiple smaller interconnected modules, each shielded individually. This approach allows for easier expansion and potentially greater resilience to damage.

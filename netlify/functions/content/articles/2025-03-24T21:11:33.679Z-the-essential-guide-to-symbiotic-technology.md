## What is Symbiotic Technology?

Symbiotic technology refers to the harmonious integration of technology and humans (or other biological systems) to create mutually beneficial relationships. Unlike traditional technology, which often operates independently, symbiotic systems are designed to work *with* us, adapting to our needs and enhancing our capabilities.  This involves a deep understanding of human-computer interaction, artificial intelligence, and the design of intuitive, seamless interfaces.  The core principle is collaboration, not control. ![A graphic depicting a human hand seamlessly interacting with a technological interface](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1488590528505-98d2b5aba04b?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress)

> **DID YOU KNOW?** The term "symbiosis" originates from biology, referring to close and long-term interactions between different biological organisms.  Symbiotic technology mirrors this concept, emphasizing mutually beneficial relationships between humans and machines.

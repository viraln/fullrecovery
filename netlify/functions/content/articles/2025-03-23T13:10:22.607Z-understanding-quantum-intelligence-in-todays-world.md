## What is Quantum Intelligence?

Quantum intelligence leverages the principles of quantum mechanics to develop algorithms and computational models that surpass the capabilities of classical computing. Unlike classical bits representing 0 or 1, quantum bits (qubits) can exist in a superposition, representing both 0 and 1 simultaneously. This, combined with quantum entanglement and other quantum phenomena, allows quantum computers to tackle problems that are intractable for even the most powerful classical supercomputers.  This enhanced computational power translates into the ability to analyze massive datasets, optimize complex systems, and create entirely new forms of artificial intelligence. ![A stylized graphic depicting qubits in superposition](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1451187580459-43490279c0fa?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress)

## Common Challenges and Solutions in Autonomous Design

Despite its potential, autonomous design faces certain challenges:

* **Data Bias:** AI models can inherit biases present in the training data, leading to skewed or unfair design outcomes.  **Solution:**  Careful curation of training data and ongoing monitoring for bias are crucial.
* **Computational Cost:**  Running complex simulations and generating numerous design options can be computationally expensive. **Solution:**  Optimization techniques and cloud computing resources can mitigate this.
* **Lack of Explainability:**  Understanding why an AI chose a particular design can be difficult. **Solution:**  The development of explainable AI (XAI) is addressing this issue.
* **Integration with Existing Workflows:** Integrating AI design tools into existing design processes can be challenging. **Solution:**  Modular design software and collaborative platforms are facilitating smoother integration.

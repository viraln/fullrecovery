## 1. The Power of Biophilic Design: Connecting with Nature

Biophilic design is a cornerstone of psychological architecture.  It leverages our innate connection to nature to create spaces that are calming, restorative, and stimulating.  This involves incorporating natural elements like plants, natural light, water features, and natural materials (wood, stone) into the design.  The aim is to create a sense of immersion in nature, even within urban environments.  Studies show that biophilic design can reduce stress, improve focus, and boost creativity.

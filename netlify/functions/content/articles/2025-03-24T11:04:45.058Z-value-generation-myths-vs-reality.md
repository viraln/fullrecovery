## Myth #1: Value is Solely Defined by Price

**Reality:** Value transcends price.  While price is a factor, true value encompasses the entire customer experience, encompassing quality, convenience, emotional connection, and long-term benefits.  A premium-priced product can be perceived as poor value if it underperforms or lacks customer support. Conversely, a lower-priced product offering exceptional service and lasting utility can deliver immense value.

> **EXPERT TIP:**  Focus on providing a holistic customer experience that goes beyond the transaction.  Think about the entire customer journey and identify opportunities to enhance value at each touchpoint.

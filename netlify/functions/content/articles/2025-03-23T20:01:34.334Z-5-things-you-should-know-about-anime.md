Anime isn't a monolithic entity; it's a diverse medium encompassing a vast array of genres and art styles. *   **Action:**  Think epic battles, superpowers, and non-stop adrenaline.  Examples include *My Hero Academia* and *Demon Slayer*.
*   **Romance:**  From sweet school romances to complex, adult relationships, anime explores the full spectrum of love.  Consider *Your Lie in April* or *Fruits Basket*.

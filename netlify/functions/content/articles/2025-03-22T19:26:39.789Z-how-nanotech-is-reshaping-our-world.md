## Carbon Nanotubes Applications: A Deep Dive

Carbon nanotubes (CNTs), cylindrical structures made of carbon atoms, are among the most promising nanomaterials. Their exceptional strength, electrical conductivity, and thermal properties make them ideal for a wide range of applications, including:

* **High-performance composites:** CNTs enhance the strength and stiffness of materials used in aerospace, automotive, and sporting goods.
* **Electronics:**  CNTs are used in transistors, sensors, and conductive inks for printed electronics.
* **Energy storage:**  CNTs improve the performance of batteries and supercapacitors.
* **Biomedical applications:** CNTs are explored for drug delivery, tissue engineering, and biosensors.

## Sustainable Habitat Recreation: Minimizing Your Impact

Sustainable practices are crucial for preserving the integrity of these valuable habitats.  Here are some key principles:

* **Leave No Trace:** Pack out everything you pack in, stay on designated trails, and minimize campfire impacts.
* **Respect Wildlife:** Observe animals from a distance, never feed them, and avoid disturbing their natural behaviors.
* **Support Local Communities:** Choose eco-friendly accommodations and businesses that prioritize sustainability.
* **Reduce Your Carbon Footprint:** Consider alternative transportation options like cycling or public transport.

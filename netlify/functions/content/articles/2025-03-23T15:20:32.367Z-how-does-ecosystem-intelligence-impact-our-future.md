## What is Ecosystem Intelligence?  A Comprehensive Definition

Ecosystem Intelligence (EI) is the process of systematically gathering, analyzing, and interpreting information about all the actors and interactions within a company's business ecosystem.  This includes partners, competitors, customers, suppliers, regulators, and even influencers.  It goes beyond simple market research; EI aims to understand the *dynamics* of the ecosystem – the relationships, power structures, and emerging trends that shape its evolution.  Unlike traditional business intelligence, which focuses internally, EI adopts a holistic, outward-looking perspective.

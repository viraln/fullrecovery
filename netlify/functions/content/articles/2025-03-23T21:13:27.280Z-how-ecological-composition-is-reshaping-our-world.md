## What is Ecological Composition?

Ecological composition, also known as ecological community composition, refers to the identity and abundance of species within a specific ecosystem. It's not just a list of species present; it encompasses the relative proportions of each species, creating a unique "fingerprint" for that particular habitat. This fingerprint is influenced by numerous factors, including climate, resource availability, disturbance regimes, and interactions between species (competition, predation, symbiosis).  Understanding species composition ecology is crucial for assessing the health and resilience of ecosystems.  ![A diverse rainforest ecosystem showcasing high ecological composition](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1451187580459-43490279c0fa?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress)

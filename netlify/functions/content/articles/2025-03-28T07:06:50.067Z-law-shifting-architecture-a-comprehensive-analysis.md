## Building Code Flexibility: Designing for the Future

Building code flexibility is essential for accommodating innovation and adapting to changing societal needs. Law-shifting architecture advocates for building codes that are performance-based rather than prescriptive, allowing architects greater freedom to design buildings that meet specific performance objectives in creative ways.

Performance-based codes focus on the desired outcomes of a building's design, such as energy efficiency, fire safety, and accessibility, rather than specifying the exact materials or methods that must be used. This allows architects to explore innovative solutions that may not be explicitly addressed in prescriptive codes.

> **EXPERT TIP:** When proposing designs that deviate from prescriptive code requirements, be prepared to provide detailed documentation and testing data to demonstrate that your design meets the required performance objectives.

## Artificial Life Examples: From Simple to Complex

ALife manifests in various forms, ranging from simple simulations to complex robotic systems.  Here are some compelling examples:

* **Genetic Algorithms:**  These algorithms mimic natural selection, allowing computer programs to evolve and adapt to changing environments.  They are widely used in optimization problems.
* **Swarm Robotics:**  Groups of simple robots interact and cooperate to achieve complex tasks, mirroring the behaviour of ant colonies or bird flocks.  ![Image of swarm robots working together](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1617917859361-3829dcd03ab6?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress)

* **Virtual Ecosystems:**  Complex simulations model entire ecosystems, allowing researchers to study ecological dynamics and the effects of environmental changes.

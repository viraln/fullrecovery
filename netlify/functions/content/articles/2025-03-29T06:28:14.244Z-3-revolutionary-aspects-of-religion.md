One of the major challenges facing religion in the 21st century is addressing issues of intolerance and extremism. Some solutions include:

*   **Promoting Interfaith Education:** Teaching individuals about different religious traditions and fostering understanding and respect.

*   **Supporting Moderate Voices:** Amplifying the voices of religious leaders and scholars who promote peace, tolerance, and dialogue.
*   **Addressing Root Causes of Extremism:** Tackling the underlying social, economic, and political factors that contribute to radicalization.

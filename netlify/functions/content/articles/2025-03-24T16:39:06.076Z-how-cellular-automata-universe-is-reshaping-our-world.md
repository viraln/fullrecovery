## Understanding the Fundamentals of Cellular Automata

Cellular automata (CA) are discrete computational models that simulate the evolution of systems over time.  They consist of a grid of cells, each in a particular state (e.g., on/off, 0/1).  The state of each cell at a given time step is determined by its own state and the states of its neighbors according to a predefined set of rules.  These rules are applied synchronously across the entire grid, creating a cascading effect that generates complex patterns from simple beginnings.  ![A simple visual representation of a cellular automata grid with cells in different states](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1451187580459-43490279c0fa?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress)

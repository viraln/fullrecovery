## Understanding the Planck Scale: A Primer

These values represent the smallest scales of length and time, and the highest energy density, that have any physical meaning within our current understanding of the universe. ![Illustration of Planck length compared to other scales](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1453686062273-6257b9fde1ce?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress)

## What is Dimensional Folding? A Deep Dive into the Concept

Dimensional folding, at its core, refers to the theoretical manipulation of spacetime to reduce the distance between two points. Instead of traveling *through* space, dimensional folding aims to alter the geometry of spacetime itself, effectively "folding" space to bring distant locations closer together.  Imagine taking a piece of paper, marking two distant points, and then folding the paper so those points touch.

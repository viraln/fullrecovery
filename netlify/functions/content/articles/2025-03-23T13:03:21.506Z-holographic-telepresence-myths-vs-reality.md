##  Understanding Holographic Telepresence: Beyond the Sci-Fi

Holographic telepresence isn't about projecting a full 3D hologram of a person like in Star Wars.  Instead, it uses advanced technologies like volumetric video capture and sophisticated display systems to create a realistic, life-sized representation of a remote participant. This representation is often displayed within a specially designed booth or on advanced screens, providing a significantly more immersive and engaging experience than traditional video conferencing.

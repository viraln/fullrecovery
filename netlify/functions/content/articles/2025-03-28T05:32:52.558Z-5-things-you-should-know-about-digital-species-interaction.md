As DSI becomes more prevalent, it's crucial to consider the ethical implications. How do we ensure the "well-being" of digital animals? Do they deserve the same consideration as real animals? These are complex questions with no easy answers.

**Key Ethical Considerations:**

*   **Digital Animal Welfare:** Should we be concerned about the "suffering" of digital animals, even if they are not sentient?

*   **Impact on Real Animals:** Could DSI lead to decreased empathy for real animals or a decline in pet ownership?

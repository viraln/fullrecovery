## What is Platform Cooperativism?

Platform cooperativism is a business model that combines the power of digital platforms with the principles of worker ownership and democratic governance.  Unlike traditional platforms dominated by venture capital and prioritizing shareholder profits, platform cooperatives prioritize the needs of their workers, users, and communities.  They are structured as cooperatives, meaning their members (workers, users, or both) collectively own and control the platform, sharing profits and decision-making power. ![A diverse group of people collaborating on a digital platform](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1690226613377-aa7c2facb58d?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress)

## Understanding the Power of Quantum Molecular Control

Quantum molecular control (QMC) harnesses the principles of quantum mechanics to precisely manipulate the behavior of molecules. Unlike classical methods, QMC leverages the wave-like nature of molecules and their interactions with tailored electromagnetic fields, primarily lasers, to steer chemical reactions and control molecular properties.

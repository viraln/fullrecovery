## Gaming for Education and Training: Gamification and Serious Games

The potential of gaming for education and training is being increasingly recognized. Gamification, the application of game-design elements and game principles in non-game contexts, is being used to make learning more engaging and effective.

*   **Gamified Learning:** Educational games and gamified learning platforms can make learning more interactive, motivating, and fun. This can lead to improved learning outcomes and increased student engagement.
*   **Serious Games:** Serious games are games designed for a primary purpose other than pure entertainment, such as training, education, or healthcare. They are being used in a wide range of fields, from military training to medical simulation to corporate training.
*   **Skill Development:** Many games require players to develop critical thinking, problem-solving, and strategic planning skills. These skills are transferable to other areas of life, such as work and education.

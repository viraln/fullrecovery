## Predictive Materials Modeling: Accelerating Discovery Through Simulation

Predictive Materials Modeling uses computational techniques to simulate the behavior of materials under different conditions. By combining these simulations with AI, we can predict material properties and performance with remarkable accuracy, reducing the need for costly and time-consuming physical experiments.

Common simulation techniques used in conjunction with MI include:

*   **Density Functional Theory (DFT):** A quantum mechanical method used to calculate the electronic structure of materials and predict their properties.
*   **Molecular Dynamics (MD):** A simulation technique that models the motion of atoms and molecules over time, allowing researchers to study the dynamic behavior of materials.
*   **Finite Element Analysis (FEA):** A numerical method used to simulate the mechanical behavior of materials under stress and strain.

## Common Challenges and Solutions

Despite its immense potential, Material Intelligence faces several challenges:

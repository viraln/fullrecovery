## Challenges and Solutions in Aerial Architecture

Designing for aerial view presents several challenges:

*   **Cost:** Implementing green roofs, solar panels, and other sustainable features can increase construction costs.
    *   **Solution:** Explore government incentives, tax breaks, and long-term cost savings associated with sustainable design.
*   **Maintenance:** Green roofs require ongoing maintenance to ensure their health and appearance.
    *   **Solution:** Incorporate automated irrigation systems and choose low-maintenance plant species.
*   **Structural Considerations:** Green roofs and solar panels can add significant weight to the roof, requiring careful structural engineering.
    *   **Solution:** Collaborate with structural engineers to ensure the building can support the added weight.
*   **Regulatory Hurdles:** Local regulations may restrict the use of certain materials or design elements.
    *   **Solution:** Research local regulations and work with planning authorities to ensure compliance.

## Future Trends in Aerial Architecture

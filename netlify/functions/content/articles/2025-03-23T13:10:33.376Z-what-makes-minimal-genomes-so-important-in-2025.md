## Understanding Minimal Genomes: The Essence of Life

A minimal genome represents the smallest set of genes necessary for a free-living organism to survive and reproduce under ideal laboratory conditions.  Think of it as the bare-bones instruction manual for life.  Scientists achieve this by systematically removing genes from existing organisms, identifying those essential for survival, and thus creating a simplified, streamlined version.

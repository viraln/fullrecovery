## What are Bio-Logic Gates?

Bio-logic gates are the biological equivalent of logic gates in traditional electronics.  Traditional logic gates, like AND, OR, and NOT, process binary information (0s and 1s).  Bio-logic gates, however, use biomolecules like DNA, RNA, or proteins to perform similar logical operations.  Instead of electrical signals, these gates use the presence or absence of specific biomolecules, binding events, or enzymatic reactions to represent 0s and 1s.  This opens up a world of possibilities for creating incredibly complex and powerful biological computing systems. ![A diagram showing a comparison between a traditional logic gate and a bio-logic gate](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1564123531124-f4ff6f8a319d?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress)

## Types of Bio-Logic Gates

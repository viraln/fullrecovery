##  FPGA Applications: Beyond the Expected

While FPGAs are already used in various applications, the next decade will see an explosion in novel use cases.  We can expect significant growth in:

## FPGA vs. ASIC: A Shifting Landscape

The traditional competition between FPGAs and ASICs (Application-Specific Integrated Circuits) will evolve. While ASICs offer superior performance and power efficiency for high-volume applications, FPGAs provide flexibility and adaptability.  We'll see a rise in hybrid approaches, combining the best features of both technologies.  This could involve using FPGAs for prototyping and low-volume production, then transitioning to ASICs for mass production.

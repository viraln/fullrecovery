Despite its potential, VR faces some challenges:

* **VR Sickness:**  This can be mitigated by starting with shorter sessions, adjusting settings like field of view, and using anti-motion sickness remedies.

* **High Cost of Entry (for some systems):**  While affordable options exist, high-end systems can be expensive.  Consider your budget and needs before making a purchase.

* **Technical Limitations:**  While technology is improving, limitations in processing power, resolution, and haptic feedback still exist.

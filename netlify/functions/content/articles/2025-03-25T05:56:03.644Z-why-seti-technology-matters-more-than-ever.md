## How SETI Works:  From Data Acquisition to Signal Analysis

The process of searching for extraterrestrial intelligence is far more complex than simply pointing a telescope at the sky. It involves a rigorous, multi-stage process:

1. **Target Selection:** Identifying promising star systems based on factors like age, stellar type, and planetary characteristics.
2. **Data Acquisition:** Using sensitive instruments to collect vast amounts of data across various wavelengths.
3. **Data Filtering:** Employing sophisticated algorithms to remove terrestrial interference and cosmic noise.

## Physical Voxel Printing: A Closer Look

Physical voxel 3D printing is a subset of additive manufacturing that focuses on the precise deposition of material to create individual voxels.  Different methods exist, including:

1. **Extrusion-based printing:**  Similar to traditional FDM 3D printing, but with significantly higher precision to create individual voxels.
2. **Stereolithography (SLA):**  Uses a laser to cure resin layer by layer, enabling high resolution and complex geometries.
3. **Digital Light Processing (DLP):**  A similar process to SLA, but uses a projector to cure the resin, allowing for faster printing speeds.

While Edge AI offers numerous benefits, there are also some challenges to consider when implementing it:

*   **Limited Resources:** Edge devices often have limited processing power, memory, and battery life.

*   **Model Deployment and Updates:** Deploying and updating AI models on a large number of edge devices can be challenging.

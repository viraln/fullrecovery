## 4. Optimizing Data Compression and Storage

In today's data-driven world, the need for efficient data compression and storage is greater than ever. Fractal geometry offers a unique approach to data compression by exploiting the self-similar patterns that often exist in images, videos, and other types of data.

Traditional compression algorithms often rely on identifying and removing redundant information. Fractal compression, on the other hand, works by identifying fractal patterns within the data and representing them using mathematical equations. This can lead to significant compression ratios, especially for images and videos with complex and repetitive patterns.

**How Fractal Compression Works:**

1.  **Divide the image into smaller blocks.**
2.  **Search for similar patterns within the image.**
3.  **Represent each block with a mathematical equation that describes the fractal transformation.**
4.  **Store the equations instead of the actual pixel data.**

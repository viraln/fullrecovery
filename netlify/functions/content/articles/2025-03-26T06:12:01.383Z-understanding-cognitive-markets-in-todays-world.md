## What are Cognitive Markets? A Definitive Definition

Cognitive markets represent a paradigm shift in how markets function.  They're not just about the exchange of goods and services; they're about leveraging cognitive technologies to optimize every aspect of that exchange.  This includes price discovery, risk assessment, fraud detection, personalized recommendations, and even the creation of entirely new market opportunities.  A cognitive market utilizes AI and ML algorithms to analyze vast datasets, identify patterns, predict future trends, and automate decision-making processes, resulting in increased efficiency, profitability, and overall market intelligence.  It's a market that "thinks" and learns, constantly adapting to changing conditions.

> **EXPERT TIP:**  The key differentiator between traditional markets and cognitive markets isn't just the *presence* of technology, but the level of *autonomy* and *intelligence* embedded within the system.

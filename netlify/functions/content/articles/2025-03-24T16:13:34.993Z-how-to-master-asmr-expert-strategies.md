## What is ASMR? Understanding the Tingling Sensation

ASMR, or Autonomous Sensory Meridian Response, is a tingling sensation that typically begins on the scalp and spreads down the back of the neck and spine. It's often triggered by specific auditory or visual stimuli, like soft whispering, gentle tapping, or slow hand movements.  ![Close-up image of a person experiencing ASMR, focusing on the subtle expression of relaxation](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1614179818750-1b4dfe94389d?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress)

While the exact neurological mechanisms behind ASMR are still being researched,  it's widely believed to be a response involving the release of endorphins and other neurochemicals associated with relaxation and well-being.

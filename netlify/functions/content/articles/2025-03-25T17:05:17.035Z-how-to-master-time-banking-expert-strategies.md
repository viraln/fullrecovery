## What is Time Banking?

Time banking is a reciprocal system where members exchange skills and services based on time units, rather than monetary value.  Think of it as a social currency where your time is the asset.  One hour of tutoring equals one hour of gardening, one hour of dog walking equals one hour of computer repair – the possibilities are limitless. ![Diverse group of people engaging in various activities within a time banking program](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1434030216411-0b793f4b4173?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress) This contrasts sharply with traditional economic models, emphasizing social connection and mutual aid over profit.

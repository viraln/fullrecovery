## What is Branching Intelligence?

Branching Intelligence is a sophisticated form of artificial intelligence that goes beyond simple linear processes.  Unlike traditional AI, which often follows a predetermined path, BI dynamically adapts its responses based on user interactions and contextual data.  It creates a network of interconnected possibilities, "branching" into different solutions based on real-time feedback.

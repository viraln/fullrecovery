## Key Concepts in Quantum Information Cosmology

Understanding QIC requires grasping a few core concepts:

*   **Quantum Entanglement:** This phenomenon, where two or more particles become linked and share the same fate, regardless of the distance separating them, plays a crucial role in QIC. *   **Quantum Entropy:** In QIC, entropy is not just a measure of disorder but also a measure of information. The entropy of the universe, particularly in its early stages, provides insights into the initial conditions and the arrow of time.
*   **Holographic Principle:** This principle suggests that the information content of a volume of space can be encoded on its boundary. *   **Quantum Gravity:** While a complete theory of quantum gravity remains elusive, QIC offers a promising avenue for exploring the quantum nature of spacetime and gravity by leveraging the principles of quantum information.

## Asteroid Deflection Techniques: A Multi-pronged Approach

Several methods are currently under investigation for deflecting asteroids:

1. **Kinetic Impactor:** This involves sending a spacecraft to collide with the asteroid, altering its trajectory through a transfer of momentum. The DART mission (2022) successfully demonstrated this technique.

2. **Gravity Tractor:** A spacecraft would use its gravitational pull to slowly nudge the asteroid off course over a long period. This method requires early detection and is best suited for smaller asteroids.

3. **Nuclear Option:** While controversial, a nuclear explosion near an asteroid could vaporize a portion of its surface, creating a thrust that alters its course. This is a last resort option for larger, more threatening asteroids.

4. **Ion Beam Shepherding:**  This innovative approach uses focused ion beams to gently nudge the asteroid over time. It's a less disruptive method compared to kinetic impactors.

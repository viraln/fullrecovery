## 1.  Proactive Problem Solving:  Predicting User Needs Before They Arise

Traditional design often reacts to user feedback and reported problems.  Anticipatory design, however, takes a radically different approach. It focuses on predicting future needs and pain points *before* they become apparent to users. This involves leveraging data analytics, behavioral science, and trend forecasting to understand emerging user behaviors and anticipate potential challenges.

> **EXPERT TIP:** Don't just analyze current user data; actively research emerging trends and technologies that could impact user needs in the near future.

This proactive approach allows designers to embed solutions directly into the product or service, creating a seamless and intuitive user experience.

## How is Cultured Meat Made? A Step-by-Step Guide

The process involves several key steps:

1. **Cell Harvesting:**  Animal cells (typically muscle stem cells) are collected via biopsy.
2. **Cell Proliferation:**  Cells are grown in a nutrient-rich medium in a bioreactor.
3. **Scaffolding (Optional):**  For more complex structures, a scaffold provides support for the growing tissue.
4. **Maturation:**  Cells differentiate and mature, forming muscle tissue.
5. **Harvesting & Processing:**  The cultured meat is harvested and processed into various cuts or products.
6. **Packaging and Distribution:**  The final product is packaged and distributed to consumers.

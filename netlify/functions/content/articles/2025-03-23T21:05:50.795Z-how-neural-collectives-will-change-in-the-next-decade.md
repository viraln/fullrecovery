## What is a Neural Collective?

A neural collective is a system of interconnected, relatively simple neural networks that collaborate to perform complex tasks. Unlike traditional AI, which relies on single, powerful models, neural collectives leverage the power of distributed computation and emergent behavior.  Think of it as a swarm of bees, each bee relatively simple, but collectively capable of building a complex hive.  Each individual network in a collective specializes in a specific subtask, contributing its expertise to the overall goal. This distributed architecture offers several key advantages, including increased robustness, scalability, and adaptability. ![Illustration of a neural collective network with interconnected nodes](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1590859808308-3d2d9c515b1a?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress)

## Mixed Reality Layers vs. Augmented Reality

While both MR and AR enhance reality with digital content, key differences exist:

| Feature          | Mixed Reality (MR)                               | Augmented Reality (AR)                             |
|-----------------|----------------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------|
| Interaction      | Full interaction with both real and digital objects | Primarily visual overlay; limited interaction      |
| Spatial Awareness | High; understands and responds to 3D environment    | Lower; often relies on 2D image recognition       |
| Occlusion        | Digital objects can occlude real-world objects       | Occlusion is often limited or absent              |
| Processing       | More computationally intensive                      | Generally less demanding                           |

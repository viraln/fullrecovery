## What is Generative Design?

Generative design, at its core, is an algorithmic approach to design.  Instead of manually creating designs, you define parameters, constraints, and objectives – and a sophisticated algorithm explores countless design iterations to find optimal solutions. This process leverages computational power to explore a vast design space far exceeding human capabilities.  Think of it as having a tireless, highly intelligent assistant who can explore millions of possibilities in minutes, identifying solutions you may never have considered. ![A visual representation of a generative design algorithm exploring various design options](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1476357471311-43c0db9fb2b4?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress)

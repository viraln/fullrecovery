## What is Epigenetics Explained?  The Science of Change

Epigenetics, simply put, is the study of heritable changes in gene expression that *do not* involve alterations to the underlying DNA sequence.  Think of it as a layer of instructions *on top* of your DNA, influencing how your genes are read and used. These instructions are influenced by environmental factors, lifestyle choices, and even experiences passed down through generations. ![A visual representation of DNA with epigenetic tags overlaid](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1723735900526-dd5931500116?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress)

Unlike genetic changes, which are permanent alterations to your DNA code, epigenetic changes are often reversible. This exciting possibility means we have a degree of control over our genetic destiny, offering a powerful path towards preventative healthcare and personalized medicine.

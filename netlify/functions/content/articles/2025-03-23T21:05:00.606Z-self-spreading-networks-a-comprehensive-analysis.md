## Self-Spreading Network Security:  Navigating the Challenges

The decentralized nature of self-spreading networks presents both opportunities and challenges in terms of security. * **Protection against malicious nodes:** Implementing robust authentication and authorization mechanisms to prevent unauthorized replication or data manipulation.

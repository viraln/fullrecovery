## Understanding Mind-Matter Interfaces: Beyond Brain-Computer Interfaces

While often conflated with brain-computer interfaces (BCIs), mind-matter interfaces encompass a broader spectrum.  BCIs primarily focus on the direct communication between the brain and external devices. MMIs, however, encompass a wider range of technologies that bridge the gap between our minds and the physical world, including BCIs but also extending to other neural interface devices that interact with the nervous system beyond just the brain.

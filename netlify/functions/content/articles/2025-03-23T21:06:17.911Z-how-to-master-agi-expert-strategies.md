## What is AGI, and How Does it Differ from AI?

Before diving into mastery strategies, let's clarify the core concept.  AGI, or artificial general intelligence, refers to a hypothetical machine with human-level intelligence.  Unlike narrow AI (like Siri or self-driving cars), which excels in specific tasks, AGI possesses the cognitive abilities to learn, reason, solve problems, and adapt to new situations across a broad range of domains.  Think of it as a truly versatile, adaptable intelligence, not confined to pre-programmed parameters.

## What is Adaptive Learning, and Why Does it Matter?

Adaptive learning leverages technology to personalize the learning experience for each individual student.  Unlike traditional methods, which deliver the same content to everyone, adaptive learning systems analyze student performance, identify strengths and weaknesses, and adjust the difficulty and content accordingly. This dynamic approach ensures that every student receives the optimal level of challenge and support, maximizing their learning potential.  ![A graphic showing a student interacting with an adaptive learning platform, highlighting personalized content and feedback.](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1472220625704-91e1462799b2?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress)

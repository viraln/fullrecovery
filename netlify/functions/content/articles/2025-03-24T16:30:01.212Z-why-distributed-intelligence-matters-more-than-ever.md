## Understanding Distributed Intelligence: Beyond Centralized Systems

Distributed intelligence (DI) represents a paradigm shift from traditional centralized systems. Instead of relying on a single, powerful central processor to handle all data and decision-making, DI distributes these tasks across a network of interconnected, autonomous agents.  These agents can be anything from sensors and IoT devices to sophisticated AI algorithms, all working collaboratively to achieve a common goal.

## Common Challenges and Solutions

One of the major challenges in cognitive enhancement is the lack of standardized assessment tools.  Accurately measuring cognitive improvement can be difficult, making it challenging to evaluate the effectiveness of different interventions.  Another challenge is the placebo effect, where individuals experience improvements simply because they believe they are receiving a beneficial treatment.  Rigorous research designs, including placebo-controlled trials, are essential to address this issue.

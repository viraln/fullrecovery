## What is Quantum Intelligence? A 2025 Perspective

Quantum intelligence, at its core, leverages the principles of quantum mechanics to enhance artificial intelligence.  Unlike classical computers that process information as bits (0 or 1), quantum computers utilize qubits. Qubits, through superposition and entanglement, can exist in multiple states simultaneously, exponentially increasing processing power. This allows quantum AI algorithms to tackle complex problems beyond the capabilities of even the most powerful classical supercomputers.  This enhanced computational power translates into breakthroughs in areas like drug discovery, materials science, and financial modeling. ![A stylized image depicting a quantum computer and its intricate workings](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1717501218456-c4789b65fc21?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress)

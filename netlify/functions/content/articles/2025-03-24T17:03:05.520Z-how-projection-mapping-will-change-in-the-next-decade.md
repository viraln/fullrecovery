## What is Projection Mapping? A Refresher for 2025

Projection mapping, also known as spatial augmented reality, is a technology that transforms ordinary surfaces into dynamic canvases. By projecting images, videos, and animations onto three-dimensional objects, it creates immersive and interactive experiences. ## Common Challenges and Solutions in Projection Mapping

Challenges include:

* **Ambient Light:**  Bright ambient light can wash out projections.  Solution: Utilize high-brightness projectors and control ambient lighting.
* **Surface Irregularities:**  Uneven surfaces can distort projections. Solution: Use advanced software with surface correction capabilities.
* **Calibration Complexity:**  Precise calibration can be time-consuming. Solution: Employ user-friendly software and seek professional assistance.

Non-Local Awareness (NLA) refers to the concept that consciousness or information can be accessed or shared regardless of physical distance or time. If you measure a property of one particle, you instantly know the corresponding property of the other, seemingly faster than light allows.

*   **Remote Viewing:** Individuals attempt to describe or draw a distant location or object without any prior knowledge or sensory input.

*   **Global Consciousness Project:** A network of random number generators worldwide, analyzed for correlations with major global events, based on the hypothesis that collective consciousness can influence physical systems.

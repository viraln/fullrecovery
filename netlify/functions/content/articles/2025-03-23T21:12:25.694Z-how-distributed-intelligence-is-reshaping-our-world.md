## What is Distributed Intelligence?

Distributed intelligence (DI) refers to a system where intelligence isn't concentrated in a single location but spread across a network of interconnected nodes.  These nodes, which can be anything from sensors and IoT devices to individual computers and even humans, collaboratively process information and make decisions.  Think of a swarm of bees working together to build a hive ‚Äì each bee contributes its small part to the overall intelligence of the colony.  Unlike centralized systems, DI is inherently resilient to failures, more adaptable to changing conditions, and capable of handling vastly larger amounts of data.

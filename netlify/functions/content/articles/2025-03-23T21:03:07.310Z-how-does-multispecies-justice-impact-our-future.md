## What is Multispecies Justice? A Definition and Exploration

Multispecies justice, at its core, is a philosophical and ethical framework that challenges anthropocentrism – the belief that humans are the central and most important beings in the universe. It argues that justice should extend beyond human society to encompass the relationships between all living beings and the ecosystems they inhabit.  This means acknowledging the inherent value of non-human life, recognizing their agency, and addressing the injustices they face due to human actions.

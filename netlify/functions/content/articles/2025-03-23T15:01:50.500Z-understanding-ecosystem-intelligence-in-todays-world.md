## What is Ecosystem Intelligence?

Ecosystem Intelligence is the process of systematically gathering, analyzing, and interpreting information about the various actors and interactions within a business's external environment.  It goes beyond traditional market research by focusing on the complex relationships and dependencies that shape the competitive landscape.  EI helps organizations understand the interconnectedness of their industry, identify emerging trends, anticipate disruptions, and make strategic decisions based on a holistic view of their ecosystem.  It involves mapping the ecosystem, identifying key players, understanding their relationships, and analyzing the overall dynamics to inform strategic planning and decision-making. ![A visual representation of a complex network illustrating an ecosystem](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1516192518150-0d8fee5425e3?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress)

## Common Challenges and Solutions

While VR offers incredible potential, it also faces challenges:

* **Motion sickness:**  Some users experience nausea or discomfort when using VR headsets.  Solutions include gradual acclimation, shorter play sessions, and improved headset design.
* **High initial investment:**  Although prices are dropping, the initial cost of a VR setup can still be a barrier for some.  Consider starting with an affordable headset and gradually upgrading.
* **Limited content:**  While the content library is growing, it's still not as extensive as other entertainment mediums.  Support independent developers and look for new releases regularly.

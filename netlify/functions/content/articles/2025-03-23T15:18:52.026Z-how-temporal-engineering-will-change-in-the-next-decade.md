## Temporal Database: The Heart of the Matter

At the core of temporal engineering lies the temporal database.  Unlike traditional databases, temporal databases explicitly track data changes over time. This allows for efficient querying of historical data, analysis of trends, and reconstruction of past states.  Several types exist, including:

## Temporal Data Modeling Techniques: Shaping the Future

Effective temporal data modeling is crucial for building robust and efficient temporal systems.  Key techniques include:

* **Valid-time modeling:** Tracks the time period during which a data value is considered valid.
* **Transaction-time modeling:** Records the time when a data value was inserted or updated into the database.
* **Bitemporal modeling:** Combines both valid-time and transaction-time modeling for a comprehensive temporal view. ![Diagram comparing valid-time, transaction-time, and bitemporal modeling](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1581092580497-e0d23cbdf1dc?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress)

## What is a Virtual Ecosystem? A 2025 Definition

A **virtual ecosystem definition** extends beyond a simple online community or platform. It's a complex, interconnected network of individuals, organizations, technologies, and resources that interact and co-evolve within a digital environment to create mutual value. These ecosystems are characterized by:

*   **Interdependence:** Participants rely on each other for resources, knowledge, and support.

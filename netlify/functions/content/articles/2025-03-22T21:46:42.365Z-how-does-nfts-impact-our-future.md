## Common Challenges and Solutions

The NFT space faces challenges:

* **High gas fees:**  Transaction costs can be expensive, particularly on Ethereum.  Solutions include exploring alternative blockchains with lower fees.
* **Scalability issues:**  Some blockchains struggle to handle the high volume of transactions.  Layer-2 scaling solutions are being developed to address this.
* **Environmental concerns:**  The energy consumption of some blockchains is a concern.  Moving to more sustainable blockchains is crucial.
* **Scams and fraud:**  The NFT space has seen its share of scams and fraudulent projects.  Due diligence and caution are essential.

## Common Challenges and Solutions: Addressing Potential Issues

While the benefits are numerous, there are some potential challenges to consider:

* **Light Pollution:**  Excessive brightness can disrupt sleep.  **Solution:**  Use dimmable lights and choose low-wattage bulbs.
* **Glare:**  Harsh light can be uncomfortable.  **Solution:**  Opt for diffused lighting solutions like lamps with shades or indirect lighting.
* **Safety Concerns:**  Improperly installed or used lights can pose a risk. **Solution:**  Always follow manufacturer instructions and choose lights with safety certifications.

##  Living Room Night Light Safety: Prioritizing Security

Safety should always be a top priority.  Here are some key safety tips:

* Choose lights with UL or CE certifications.
* Avoid overloading electrical outlets.
* Keep cords out of reach of children and pets.
* Regularly inspect lights for damage.
* Never leave lights unattended when using candles or other open flames.

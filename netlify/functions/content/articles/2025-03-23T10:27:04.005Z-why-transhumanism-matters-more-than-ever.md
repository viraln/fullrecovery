## Transhumanism vs. Posthumanism: A Key Distinction

While often used interchangeably, transhumanism and posthumanism represent distinct concepts. Transhumanism focuses on enhancing human capabilities using technology, aiming to improve the *existing* human condition.  Posthumanism, on the other hand, envisions a future where humans have fundamentally transformed, potentially surpassing their biological limitations to such an extent that they are no longer recognizable as human.  This could involve merging with AI, achieving digital immortality, or evolving into entirely new forms of life.  ![A comparative table illustrating the differences between Transhumanism and Posthumanism](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1517976487492-5750f3195933?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress)

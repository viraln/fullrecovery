## What is Adaptive Learning?

Adaptive learning, at its core, is a personalized learning experience tailored to the individual's pace, style, and needs.  Unlike traditional methods that deliver the same content to everyone, adaptive learning systems utilize sophisticated algorithms and data analysis to adjust the learning path in real-time. ![A graphic illustrating the difference between traditional and adaptive learning pathways](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1472220625704-91e1462799b2?q=85&w=1200&fit=max&fm=webp&auto=compress)  These systems assess a learner's strengths and weaknesses, identify knowledge gaps, and dynamically adjust the difficulty and content to optimize learning outcomes. This means no more struggling with material that's too easy or falling behind with content that's too difficult.
